<h2 class="anchor anchorWithStickyNavbar_fMI7" id="background">Background<a class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Background" title="Direct link to Background" href="/docs/babt/introduction#background">​</a></h2>
<p>Soulbound Token (SBT)  is a new concept proposed by Vitalik Buterin recently(Soul Bound idea and the recent paper about decentralized society). It will be a keystone for Web3 to go to the next level, &quot;Decentralized Society&quot; (DeSoc).</p>
<p>SBTs are most like NFTs we see today, but they have two key different properties.</p>
<ol>
<li>Non-transferable - When someone gets an SBT, He/She cannot transfer this SBT to another address.</li>
<li>Revocable - SBTs can be revoked by issuers.</li>
</ol>
<p>Suppose Binance issues Binance Account Bound(BAB) tokens which are credentials of Binance KYC users. So here are the potential scenarios to use these tokens:</p>
<ol>
<li>A new protocol only airdrops to addresses with BAB tokens since business cooperation.</li>
<li>A NFT only allows minting from addresses with BAB tokens to avoid bots.</li>
<li>A DAO only allows voting from addresses with  BAB tokens, and each such address will have a cap of voting power to ensure governance fairness.</li>
<li>A DAO uses Quadratic Voting, and only allows addresses with BAB tokens.</li>
</ol>
<h2 class="anchor anchorWithStickyNavbar_fMI7" id="purpose">Purpose<a class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Purpose" title="Direct link to Purpose" href="/docs/babt/introduction#purpose">​</a></h2>
<p>SBTs are still in the stage of concept. We have two purposes for this pilot project.</p>
<ol>
<li>Accumulate the influence in the cutting-edge technology field of the chain world.</li>
<li>Introduce some new elements to promote the ecological prosperity of BSC.</li>
</ol>
<h2 class="anchor anchorWithStickyNavbar_fMI7" id="goal">Goal<a class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Goal" title="Direct link to Goal" href="/docs/babt/introduction#goal">​</a></h2>
<p>We will give a practice about SBT by issuing BAB tokens. Users can mint, revoke and check BAB tokens on the Binance platform.</p>
