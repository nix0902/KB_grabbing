# Inception date 

#### What is it?
 The Inception date is when an ETF was officially launched and began trading. This provides insight into the fund’s longevity.

#### Why is it important?

Older ETFs with a long track record may be perceived as more reliable and established. They may have more investor confidence and higher liquidity.
