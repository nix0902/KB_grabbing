# Offer price performance % 
 What is Offer price performance %?

Offer price performance measures how much an IPO stock’s price has changed compared to its initial offering price. It is calculated as:

Offer price performance = (Current price - Offer price) / Offer price × 100%

Why is it important?

This metric shows whether the stock has traded above or below its offer price since it began trading publicly, indicating positive or negative performance.
