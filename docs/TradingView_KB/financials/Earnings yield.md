Earnings yield is the ratio of [Diluted earnings per share](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553616) over the trailing twelve months (TTM) to the company’s share price. Formula: Diluted earnings per share TTM / Stock price What does Earnings yield mean? Earnings yield shows how much the company has earned per share as a percentage of its share price. It shows investors how much yield they are getting in earnings in return for owning the stock at its current share price.
