- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Financials - / [ Crypto ](/support/folders/43000590536-crypto/) - / [ In the money addresses % ](/support/solutions/43000703316-in-the-money-addresses/) # In the money addresses % What is “In the money addresses %”? In the money is an indicator which identifies the average price of token purchasing and compares it with current price. Accounts, with current price of token &gt; average price of purchase called “In the money”. Why is “In the money addresses %” important ? “In the money” indicator shows the proportion of the account with profit. If the percentage of such accounts is too large, then some investors may decide to cash out their profits, which will lead to lower prices.
