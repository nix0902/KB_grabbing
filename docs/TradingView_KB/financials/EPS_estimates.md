# EPS estimates 

#### What are EPS estimates?
 This is the estimated future Earnings divided by the current number of shares. Future earnings estimates are often provided by the company itself or by financial analysts.

#### What does EPS estimates mean?

This metric shows what analysts believe a company’s future [Earnings per share](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563520) will be. Analysts are not always correct, so sometimes their estimates are incorrect. EPS estimates are used to predict how well a company is performing and where its future earnings will be.
