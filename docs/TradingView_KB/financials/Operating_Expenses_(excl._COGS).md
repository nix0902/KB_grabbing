# Operating Expenses (excl. COGS) 

#### What is Operating Expenses (excl. COGS)?
 Operating Expenses (excl. COGS) represents expenses a business incurs through its normal business operations, but excluding [Costs Of Goods Sold](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553618). The last is already included in [Gross Profit](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553611).

#### How is Operating Expenses (excl. COGS) calculated?

Operating Expenses (excl. COGS) is the sum of [Selling, General & Administrative Expenses, Total](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553613) and [Other Operating Expenses](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563483).
