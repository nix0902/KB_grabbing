Short Term Investments, Total represent investments that are expected to be sold or converted into cash in the next 3 to 12 months. What do Short Term Investments, Total include? - Available-for-sale investments classified as current; - Bonds, and other fixed income securities, classified as current; - Derivative assets classified as current; - Held-to-maturity investments expected to mature within the current cycle; - Marketable securities classified as current; - Mutual funds classified as current and expected to be disposed of; and - Other financial assets classified as current.
