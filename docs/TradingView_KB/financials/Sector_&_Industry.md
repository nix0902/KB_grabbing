# Sector & Industry 
 The *FactSet Industries and Economic Sectors* model is used to classify companies into sectors and industries. This is a proprietary model that employs a hierarchical structure, where companies are grouped based on the business that generates the majority of their revenue. This approach allows for a more accurate classification of diversified companies. 

Sectors in *F**actSet Industries and Economic Sectors* are organized into several general economic categories:

- [Durables](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000724515) (long-term use goods)
- [Non-durables](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000724516) (short-term use goods)
- [Services](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000724517)
- [Infrastructure](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000724518)
- [Miscellaneous](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000724519)
