Sale/Maturity of Investments represents an increase in funds from sales and maturity of investments. This includes both short-term and long-term investments. It includes: - Decrease in (the sale of) investment property; - Decrease in short-term investments; - Decrease in marketable securities; - Decrease in and/or maturity of securities; and - Decrease in and/or maturity of bonds.
