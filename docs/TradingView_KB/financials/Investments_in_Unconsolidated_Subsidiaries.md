# Investments in Unconsolidated Subsidiaries 

#### What are Investments in Unconsolidated Subsidiaries?
 Investments in Unconsolidated Subsidiaries represent a company’s unconsolidated subsidiaries, including companies accounted for using the equity method, joint ventures and other unconsolidated subsidiaries are held at cost.

#### What do Investments in Unconsolidated Subsidiaries include?

- Investments in associated companies;
- Investments in unconsolidated subsidiaries;
- Investments in joint ventures;
- Goodwill arising from investments in associates; and
- Loans and advances to associated/affiliated companies.
