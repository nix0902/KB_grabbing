# Traders / Unique buyers / Unique sellers 

#### What are Traders?
 Traders are unique blockchain addresses that act as buyers or sellers of tokens within a pool on a specific decentralized exchange.

#### Why is it important to know the number of Traders/Buyers/Sellers?

A high number of Traders in the pool may indicate its activity, and the ratio of the number of Buyers and Sellers indicates the price dynamics in the pool.
