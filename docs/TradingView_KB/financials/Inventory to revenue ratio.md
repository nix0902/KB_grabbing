Inventory to revenue is calculated as the average of [Inventories](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563745) over the past two periods divided by [Revenue](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553619). It is not calculated for banks. Formula: Average inventories / Revenue What does Inventory to revenue ratio mean? It measures the amount of inventory that a company has to maintain its current revenue. It is an indicator of a company's ability to manage its stocks.
