Price to operating cash flow is the ratio of the share price to Operating cash flow. Essentially, Price to operating cash flow measures how much money a company generates relative to its share price. Formula: Price to cash flow ratio = Price close * Common shares outstanding / [Cash from operating activities](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553627) What does Price to cash flow ratio mean? Price to cash flow is considered to be a more indicative investment measurement metric than Price to earnings per share because cash flows cannot be manipulated as easily as incomes.
