# Capital Expenditures, Total 

#### What are Capital expenditures, Total?
 Capital expenditures, total represent funds used by a company to acquire, upgrade and maintain physical assets such as property, plant and equipment.

They are the sum of [Capital Expenditures - Fixed Assets](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564167) and [Capital Expenditures - Other Assets](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564168).

#### What does CapEx per share show?

CapEx per share shows how much capital expenditure is allocated per share. It can highlight how aggressively a company is investing in future growth or maintaining existing infrastructure. High CapEx per share may suggest expansion, but could also pressure cash flow.
