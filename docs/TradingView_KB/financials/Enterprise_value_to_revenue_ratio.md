# Enterprise value to revenue ratio 

#### What is Enterprise value to revenue ratio?
 The Enterprise value to revenue ratio counts as an [Enterprise Value](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000597077) divided by [Revenue](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553619).

#### Formula:

Enterprise value / Revenue

#### What does Enterprise value to revenue ratio mean?

It helps to compare a company's revenue with the company’s enterprise value. The lower it is, the better as it may be a sign that a company is underestimated or undervalued.
