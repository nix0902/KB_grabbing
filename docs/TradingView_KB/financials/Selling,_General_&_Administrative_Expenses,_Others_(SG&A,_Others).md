# Selling, General & Administrative Expenses, Others (SG&A, Others) 

#### What does SG&A mean?
 Selling, General & Administrative Expenses represent the expenses that are not directly related to a company’s production process, but instead represent the costs related to sales, general, and administrative functions that keep a company running. [Total SG&A](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553613) also includes [Research & Development](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553612) costs while SG&A, Others excludes [Research & Development](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553612) costs.

#### How is SG&A, Others calculated?

SG&A, Others is calculated by finding the difference between [Total SG&A](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553613) and [Research & Development](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553612).
