Capital Expenditures - Other Assets represent cash outlays made to acquire, or maintain assets classified as long-term, aside from Property, Plant and Equipment and investments. They include an: - Increase in Exploration and Evaluation Assets; - Increase Intangible Assets; and - Increase in Deferred Charges.
