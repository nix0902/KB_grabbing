# Change in Accounts Receivable 

#### What is Change in Accounts Receivable?
 Change in Accounts Receivable represents change in working capital due to increase or decrease in receivables. This is included in the [Changes in Working Capital](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564147).
