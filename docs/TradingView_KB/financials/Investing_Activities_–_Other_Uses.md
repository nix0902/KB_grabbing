# Investing Activities – Other Uses 

#### What are Investing Activities – Other Uses?
 Investing Activities (Other Uses) represent any other funds used for investing activities other than [Capital Expenditures](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564166), [Purchase of Investments](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564162) and [Purchase/Acquisition of Business](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564159).
