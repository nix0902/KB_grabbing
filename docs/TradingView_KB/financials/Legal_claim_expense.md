# Legal claim expense 

#### What is Legal claim expense?
 Legal claim expense represents the income or losses resulting from litigation.

Only annual data are available. 

It includes:

- Bankruptcy fee;
- Lawsuit expense;
- Lawsuit provision and expense, when combined and cannot be separated;
- Legal disputes;
- Reimbursement of legal costs;
- Settlement income.
