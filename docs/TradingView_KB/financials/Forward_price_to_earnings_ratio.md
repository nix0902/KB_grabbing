# Forward price to earnings ratio 

#### What is Forward price to earnings ratio?
 Forward price to earnings ratio is the ratio of the value of a share to the predicted Earnings per share for a given period.

#### What does Forward price to earnings ratio mean?

Forward price to earnings ratio is used to evaluate a company today relative to its forecasted Total earnings that are expected or estimated to come in future time periods.
