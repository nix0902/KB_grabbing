# Change in Accrued Expenses 

#### What is Change in Accrued Expenses?
 Change in Accrued Expenses represents change in working capital due to increase or decrease in accrued expenses, excluding income taxes, and accrued income. This is included in the [Changes in Working Capital](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564147).
