# Other exceptional charges 

#### What is Other exceptional charges?
 Other exceptional charges represent the part of Unusual income/expense that is not specified as being any of the other items.

Only annual data are available.

It includes:

- Restructuring of debt;
- Early termination of contract;
- Acquired In-Process R&D;
- Expenses (income) from calamitous events;
- Accelerated depreciation, and other.
