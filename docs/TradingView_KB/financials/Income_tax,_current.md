# Income tax, current 

#### What is Income tax, current?
 Income tax, current represents the total amount of tax liability due to the government where the company is domiciled. For U.K. companies, relief for overseas taxation is netted against the tax expense.

#### How is Income tax, current calculated?

Income tax, current is the sum of Income tax, current - domestic and Income tax, current - foriegn.
