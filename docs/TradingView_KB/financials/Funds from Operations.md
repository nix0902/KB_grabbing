Funds from Operations represent the gross cash flow from a company’s operations, derived by reconciling net income before the effects of non-cash items. These are included in the [Cash from Operating Activities](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553627). How are Funds from Operations calculated? Funds from Operations are the sum of: - [Net Income (Cash Flow)](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563888); - [Depreciation & Amortization (Cash Flow)](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563892); - [Deferred Taxes (Cash Flow)](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564144); and - [Non-Cash Items.](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564146) Previous Previous Cash From Operating Activities Next Next Net Income (Cash Flow) Launch Supercharts
