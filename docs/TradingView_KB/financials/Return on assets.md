ROA is calculated as [Net income before discontinued operations](https://www.tradingview.com/chart/?solution=43000563500) by the average of [Total assets](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553623) over the past two periods. If the denominator is negative, then the field value will be empty. For quarterly ROA, the calculations take the LTM value of Net income. Formula: Net income before discontinued operations / Total average assets What does ROA mean? It gives an idea of how efficiently a company’s assets are used to make a profit. A high ROA indicates that a company successfully converts invested money into income. It may vary markedly depending on the industry.
