A transaction is an operation of buying or selling a token within a pool on a specific decentralized exchange. Why is it important to know the number of Transactions/Buys/Sells? It is interesting to know the number of buys relative to sells to determine the dynamics of price movement within the pool. For example, let's imagine a WBTC/WETH pool in which someone buys WBTC for WETH. As a result, the supply of WBTC in the pool decreases, and WETH increases, eventually leading to an increase in the price of WBTC relative to WETH.
