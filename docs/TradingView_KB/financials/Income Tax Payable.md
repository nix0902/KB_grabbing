Income Tax Payable represents all the company’s taxes on income due to governments, accrued and payable, within the current period. What does Income Tax Payable include? - Accrued income tax; - Income/corporation tax payable; - Current deferred taxes; - Withholding tax; and - Other taxes, if they cannot be separated.
