The Free cash flow margin is the ratio of [Free cash flow](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553630) to [Total revenue](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553619). Formula: Free cash flow margin = Free Cash Flow / Total Revenue What does Free cash flow margin mean? The Free cash flow margin is a measure of how efficiently a company converts its sales to cash. The higher the percentage, the more cash is available from sales. A company that shows an increasing cash flow margin from year to year is certainly getting stronger with time.
