# Other Costs of Goods Sold (Other COGS) 

#### What are Other COGS?
 Other COGS represent the direct costs of production and/or the costs of services rendered, excluding [Depreciation and Amortization](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563477), which are incurred in the revenue generation process during the period.
