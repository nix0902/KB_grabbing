# Gross Property/Plant/Equipment 

#### What is Gross Property/Plant/Equipment?
 Gross Property/Plant/Equipment represents the aggregate historical or fair value of complete or under development, acquired or self-constructed, delivered or in transit, long-lived tangible assets that are used or intended to be used in the company’s operations.
