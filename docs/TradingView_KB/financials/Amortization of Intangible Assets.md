Amortization of Intangible Assets represents amortization charges for the period against intangible assets, reported as non-cash adjustment in order to reconcile net income to cash flows. Thise is derived from a company’s operations. They include: - Amortization of deferred charges; - Amortization of goodwill; - Amortization of club membership classified as intangible asset; and - Amortization of land use.
