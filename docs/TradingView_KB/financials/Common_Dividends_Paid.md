# Common Dividends Paid 

#### What are Common Dividends Paid?
 Common Dividends Paid represent the total cash dividends to common shareholders of the company.

They include:

- Distributions to REIT unit holders;
- Distributions to partners;
- Distributions to hybrid capital;
- Dividend equivalents (payments-in-lieu of dividends) to restricted stock units;
- Dividends paid to profit-participating preferred shares.
