Interest expense is a non-operating expense shown on the income statement. It represents interest payable on borrowings. How is Interest Expense, Net of Interest Capitalized calculated? This is calculated as [Interest Expense on Debt](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563467) minus [Interest Capitalized](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563468).
