# Paid in Capital 

#### What is Paid in Capital?
 Paid in Capital represents the sum of [Common Stock Par/Carrying Value](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563873), [Additional Paid-in Capital/Capital Surplus](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563874) and [Treasury Stock](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563875).
