# Investing Activities – Other Sources 

#### What are Investing Activities – Other Sources?
 Investing Activities (Other Sources) represent increase in funds generated from investing activities other than [Sale of Fixed Assets & Businesses](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564158) and [Sale/Maturity of Investments](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564161).
