# Issuers, Brands, and Providers 

#### Issuer for ETFs
 *Issuer* is the entity responsible for creating and managing the ETF. This institution ensures the ETF meets all regulatory requirements and aligns with its stated investment objective.

#### Brand of ETFs

Typically, it refers to the recognizable name or firm that markets the ETF. The brand can influence investors' perceptions of the ETF's quality and reliability.

#### Index Provider for ETFs

The organization that creates and maintains the benchmark index, which the ETF aims to track. The index provider determines the rules for index composition and its periodic rebalancing.

#### Index Tracked for ETFs

The specific benchmark index an ETF seeks to replicate, guiding its asset selection and performance objectives.
