# Income tax, deferred - domestic 

#### What is Income tax, deferred - domestic?
 Income tax, deferred - domestic represents the portion of deferred taxes due the government of the country where the company is domiciled that have been expensed during the year.

Only annual data are available.

It includes:

- Deferred taxes;
- Prior year adjustments of deferred taxation.
