Long term debt to total assets ratio counts as a [Long-term debt](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553621) divided by [Total assets](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553623). Formula: Long-term debt & Capital lease obligation / Total assets What does Long term debt to total assets ratio mean? It is an indicator of company assets that are financed by long-term loans and financial liabilities.
