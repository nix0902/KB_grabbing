# Change in Taxes Payable 

#### What is Change in Taxes Payable?
 Change in Taxes Payable represents the change in working capital due to increase or decrease in income tax payable. This is included in the [Changes in Working Capital](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564147).
