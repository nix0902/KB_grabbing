# Issuer сountry 
 Issuer сountry is the country where the legal entity that issued a bond is registered. For corporate bonds, this is the country of registration of the issuing company; for sovereign bonds, it represents the issuing government’s country.

For corporate issuers, this indicator is functionally equivalent to [Country of registration](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000754102).
