The amount of messages with positive connotation in top Telegram groups for a particular crypto. What is “Telegram negative”? The amount of messages with negative connotation in top Telegram groups for a particular crypto. Why is “Telegram sentiment” important? “Telegram sentiment” measures the amount of positive/negative messages or reactions for particular crypto in Telegram groups. Sentiment indicators try to measure how bullish or bearish investors feel towards markets or particular crypto. In other words, by using sentiment indicators, we are trying to understand what investors are thinking and feeling or what can help forecast their future behavior.
