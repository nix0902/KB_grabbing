# Total Cash Dividends Paid 

#### What are Total Cash Dividends Paid?
 Total Cash Dividends Paid represent the total dividends paid to common and preferred shareholders of a company for the period. They include [Common Dividends Paid](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564185) and [Preferred Dividends Paid](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564186).
