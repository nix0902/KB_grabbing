# Preferred Dividends 

#### What are Preferred Dividends?
 These represent actual cash dividend payments on preferred stocks or provisions for preferred dividends, if in arrears. It also includes accretion on preferred stock. If a company has ESOP preferred stock, the dividend will be shown net of the tax benefit.
