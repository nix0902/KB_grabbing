- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Financials - / [ Statistics ](/support/folders/43000572993-statistics/) - / [ Dividends per share growth % ](/support/solutions/43000734974-dividends-per-share-growth/) # Dividends per share growth % Dividends per share (DPS) growth is a financial metric that measures the percentage change in [dividends per share](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000670334) over specific time periods. This metric provides insight into how a company's dividend payments to shareholders have changed over time. Currently, only Annual YoY (Year-over-Year) is available for this metric. DPS growth helps assess the company's dividend policy and its commitment to returning value to shareholders through dividends. This metric would provide the percentage change in dividends per share from one year to the next, indicating the growth or decline in dividend payments to shareholders over the specified annual period. Positive percentage growth indicates an increase in DPS, while negative percentage growth suggests a decrease in DPS compared to the previous year.
