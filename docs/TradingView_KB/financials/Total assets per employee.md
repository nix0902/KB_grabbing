- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Financials - / [ Statistics ](/support/folders/43000572993-statistics/) - / [ Total assets per employee ](/support/solutions/43000734966-total-assets-per-employee/) # Total assets per employee Total assets per employee is a financial metric that measures the amount of [total assets](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553623) held by a company [per employee](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000597080). Total assets represent all the resources owned by a company, including cash, inventory, property, equipment, and investments. Dividing the total assets by the number of employees provides insight into how much value in assets the company holds per employee in a year. Total assets per employee = Total assets / Number of employees The result of this calculation provides the amount of total assets owned by the company per employee on an annual basis. This metric helps assess the asset efficiency of a company in relation to its workforce. A higher total assets per employee value indicates that the company has a significant amount of assets per employee, which can be a positive indicator of the company's scale and asset base. Comparing total assets per employee across companies can provide insights into each company's asset management strategy and operational efficiency.
