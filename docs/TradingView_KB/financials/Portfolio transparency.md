Portfolio transparency refers to the extent to which an ETF discloses the specifics of its holdings to investors. Types of Portfolio Transparency: Transparent : These ETFs reveal their holdings on a daily basis, providing clear insight into their asset composition. Semi or non-transparent : These ETFs disclose their holdings less frequently or with less detail, often to protect the strategies of actively managed funds.
