- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Financials - / [ Statistics ](/support/folders/43000572993-statistics/) - / [ Working capital per share ](/support/solutions/43000761667-working-capital-per-share/) # Working capital per share Working Capital per Share measures the amount of short-term capital available to the company on a per-share basis, calculated as (Current Assets – Current Liabilities) divided by the number of outstanding shares. It provides insight into the company’s liquidity position and operational efficiency from a shareholder perspective. A higher value typically indicates that the company has sufficient resources to cover its short-term obligations and continue day-to-day operations without liquidity stress. This metric can be especially helpful for evaluating financial health in capital-intensive or seasonal businesses where working capital needs may fluctuate.
