Forward price to sales is the ratio of a company’s current share price to the estimated future value of Sales per Share. What does Forward price to sales ratio mean? Forward price to sales ratio is used to evaluate a company today relative to its forecasted Total revenues that are expected or estimated to come in future time periods.
