# Income tax, deferred 

#### What is Income tax, deferred?
 Income tax, deferred returns total deferred income tax for the period and date(s) requested in local currency by default.

#### How is Income tax, current calculated?

Income tax, deferred is calculated as the sum of Income tax, deferred - domestic and Income tax, deferred - foreign.
