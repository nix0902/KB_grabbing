This parameter shows the ratio of [Goodwill](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563688) compared to the total level of a company's assets. Using this relationship, you can see how much of a company’s intangible assets are occupied by their total number. Formula: Goodwill / [Total assets](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553623) What does Goodwill to assets ratio mean? A small value will indicate a large number of tangible assets that a company can sell for money. A large value may indicate a large number of intangible assets that are more difficult to sell for cash value in the event of bankruptcy.
