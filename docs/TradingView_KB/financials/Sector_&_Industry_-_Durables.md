# Sector & Industry - Durables 
 According to the* FactSet Industries and Economic Sectors* classification, the *Durables* category includes sectors that produce consumer goods with a longer lifespan, such as motor vehicles, home furnishings, electronics, appliances, tools, recreational products, and other consumer specialties. These sectors primarily focus on manufacturing products for the consumer market that are designed to last for an extended period.
