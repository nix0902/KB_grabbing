# Days sales outstanding 

#### What is the Days sales outstanding?
 Days Sales Outstanding Ratio counts as [Accounts Receivable](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563740) divided by [Revenue](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553619) and multiplied by the number of days in the period. It is not calculated for banks.

#### Formula:

Accounts receivable / Revenue * Days in period

#### What does the Days sales outstanding mean?

This value is the average number of days spent on revenue after sales. Thus, it shows how successfully a company operates with receivables.
