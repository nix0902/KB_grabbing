Diluted Net Income represents Net Income that is adjusted by Dilution Adjustment for Diluted EPS computation. Diluted Net Income assumes the conversion of all convertible preferred stock and debt, which means the net income will be adjusted for not paying out any interest expense or preferred dividends. How is Diluted Net Income Available to Common Stockholders calculated? The Net Income Available to Common Fully Diluted EPS is calculated as [Net Income](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553617) plus [Dilution Adjustment](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563504) minus [Preferred Dividends](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563506).
