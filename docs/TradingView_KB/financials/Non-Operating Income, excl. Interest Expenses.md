Interest Expenses ](/support/solutions/43000563471-non-operating-income-excl-interest-expenses/) # Non-Operating Income, excl. Interest Expenses What is Non-Operating Income, excl. Interest Expenses? Non-Operating Income, excl. Interest Expenses represents all non-operating income/expenses besides Unusual Expenses and Interest Expenses. How is Non-Operating Income, excl. Interest Expense calculated? This is calculated as [Non-Operating Interest Income](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563473) plus [Pretax Equity In Earnings](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563474) plus [Other Income/Expense](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563479).
