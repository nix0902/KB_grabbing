It represents the net income margin for the requested period and date. This is calculated as [Net Income](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553617) divided by [Revenue](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553619) multiplied by 100. Formula: Net income / Revenue * 100% What does Net margin mean? Using this parameter, you can evaluate whether a company’s management brings significant profit from its sales, and whether operating and overhead costs are controlled. For example, if a company has growing income, but its operating expenses simultaneously grow faster than income, then its net profit will decrease.
