The number of members in the crypto official Telegram group. Why are “Telegram members” important? The number of members in a crypto asset’s Telegram group is reflective of its community (though there can be ways to game this metric). In general, the number of Telegram members tends to be a lagging indicator of price, meaning that it tends to move along with price but with a delayed time.
