# Issuance (Retirement) of Long Term Debt 

#### What is Issuance (Retirement) of Long Term Debt?
 Issuance (Retirement) of Long Term Debt represents the net cash flow from long-term debt for the period. This is the sum of [Issuance of Long Term Debt](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564176) and [Reduction of Long Term Debt](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564177).
