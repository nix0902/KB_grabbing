# Price to tangible book ratio 

#### What is Price to tangible book ratio?
 Price to Tangible book is the ratio of stock price to tangible book value.

#### Formula:

Price to tangible book = Price close / [Tangible book value per share](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000597072)

#### What does Price to tangible book ratio mean?

Price to tangible book ratio shows the amount of money that an investor would receive for each share if a company ceased operations and liquidated all its assets at the value recorded in the company's books.
