# Other Income/Expense 

#### What is Other Income/Expense?
 Other Income/Expense represents other [Non-Operating Income, excluding Interest Expenses](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563471) besides [Non-Operating Interest Income](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563473), and [Pretax Equity In Earnings](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563474).

It includes:

- Gain/Loss on disposal of assets
- Dividend income - non-operating
- Foreign exchange transactions
- Monetary correction
- Discontinued operations - pre-tax
- Foreign currency translation gain/loss
- Allowance for funds used during construction, if not interest related (Utility companies only)
- Gain on sale of investment (Other financial companies only)
