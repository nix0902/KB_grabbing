- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Financials - / [ ETF ](/support/folders/43000592471-etf/) - / [ Dividend treatment ](/support/solutions/43000720269-dividend-treatment/) # Dividend treatment Dividends in ETFs represent a portion of the profits distributed to shareholders. There are two main approaches to handling these dividends: - Distributes : Dividends are paid out to shareholders in cash, typically monthly, quarterly, or annually. This option provides immediate income but may have tax implications. - Reinvests : Instead of cash payouts, dividends are reinvested in purchasing additional ETF shares. This approach promotes long-term investment growth through compound interest but does not provide immediate income to shareholders.
