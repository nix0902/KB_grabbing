# Long Term Investments 

#### What are Long Term Investments?
 A long-term investment is an account on the asset side of a company's balance sheet that represents the company's investments held for more than one year or accounting period.

#### How are Long Term Investments calculated?

Long Term Investments are the sum of [Note Receivable - Long Term](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563641), [Investments in Unconsolidated Subsidiaries](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563645) and [Other Investments](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563649).
