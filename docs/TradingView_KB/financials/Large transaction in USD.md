The volume of transactions in USD of a particular crypto with a value of $100,000 or greater. Why is a “Large transaction in USD” important? Since this sum of money is not available to the average retail trader, the “Large transactions in USD” indicator will allow investors to track the transactions of major market players and focus on them when making investment decisions.
