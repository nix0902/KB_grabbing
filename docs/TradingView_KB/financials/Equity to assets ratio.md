Equity to assets ratio counts as [Total stockholders equity](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000557442) divided by [Total assets](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553623). Formula: Total stockholders equity / Total assets What does Equity to assets ratio mean? It shows the ratio of the share of assets owned by shareholders to the total amount of assets.
