# Operating Lease Liabilities 

#### What are Operating Lease Liabilities?
 Operating Lease Liabilities represents long-term portion of obligations under operating lease agreement reported either as a stand-alone item within total long-term liabilities or as a breakdown of other long-term liabilities.
