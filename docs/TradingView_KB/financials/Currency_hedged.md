# Currency hedged 
 Currency hedging impacts risk management in ETFs.

*Hedged* ETFs use financial instruments to reduce the impact of currency exchange rate fluctuations on investment returns. The goal is to decrease volatility and increase the stability of returns.

*Unhedged* ETFs do not employ hedging measures, leaving the fund's performance subject to currency market fluctuations. This approach entails higher currency risk but also offers the potential to benefit from favorable exchange rate movements.
