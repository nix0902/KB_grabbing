This ratio allows you to evaluate a company's ability to make profit in comparison with the market, taking into account its Balance Sheet. Formula: [Enterprise value](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000597077) / EBIT What does Enterprise value to EBIT ratio mean? This ratio can be considered an improved version of [Price to earnings ratio](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000597017), because it contains a larger number of company parameters, especially in industries where companies have large amounts of debt. A high ratio indicates that the company's shares are overvalued, and a low one may indicate their undervaluation.
