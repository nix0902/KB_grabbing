# Change in Accounts Payable 

#### What is Change in Accounts Payable?
 Change in Accounts Payable represents the change in working capital due to increase or decrease in accounts payable. This is included in the [Changes in Working Capital](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564147).
