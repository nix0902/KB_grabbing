- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Financials - / [ Bonds ](/support/folders/43000594677-bonds/) - / [ Issuer сountry ](/support/solutions/43000754104-issuer-ountry/) # Issuer сountry Issuer сountry is the country where the legal entity that issued a bond is registered. For corporate bonds, this is the country of registration of the issuing company; for sovereign bonds, it represents the issuing government’s country. For corporate issuers, this indicator is functionally equivalent to [Country of registration](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000754102).
