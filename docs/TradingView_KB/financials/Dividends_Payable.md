# Dividends Payable 

#### What are Dividends Payable?
 Dividends Payable represent all dividends to common shareholders proposed by the board, but not yet paid or appropriated in [Shareholder’s Equity](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000557442). In addition, dividends payable represent accumulated, unpaid dividends due to shareholders of preferred stock, which are expected to be distributed within the current period.
