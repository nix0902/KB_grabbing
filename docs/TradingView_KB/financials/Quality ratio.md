This is the general attitude of [Gross income](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553611) towards [Total assets](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553623). What does Quality ratio mean? The Quality ratio shows how efficiently a company uses its total assets to make a profit. Companies with high rates usually have enough income to invest in marketing and in their own development.
