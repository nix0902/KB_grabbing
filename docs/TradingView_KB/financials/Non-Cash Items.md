- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Financials - / [ Cash Flow ](/support/folders/43000565037-cash-flow/) - / [ Non-Cash Items ](/support/solutions/43000564146-non-cash-items/) # Non-Cash Items What are Non-Cash Items? Non-Cash Items represent all non-cash adjustments to reconcile net income to cash flows derived from the company’s operations not otherwise and elsewhere here classified. They include: - Interest paid; - Interest received; - Income taxes paid or refunded; - Gains/losses on the disposal of assets; - Changes in provisions (i.e. doubtful accounts, pension, risks and charges); - Minority interest; - Unusual expenses reported as non-cash adjustment; - Valuation of financial instruments; - Share of income/loss from associated companies; - Foreign exchange gain/loss; and - Amortization of grants.
