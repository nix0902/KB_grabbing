# Amortization of deferred charges 

#### What is Amortization of deferred charges?
 Amortization of deferred charges refers to the systematic allocation of capitalized expenditures to the income statement over their useful economic lives. This item is non-cash in nature.

Only annual data are available.

It includes:

- Amortization of business development costs;
- Amortization of other deferred charges.
