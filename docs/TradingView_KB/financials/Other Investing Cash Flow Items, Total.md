Other Investing Cash Flow Items, Total represent the [Cash From Investing Activities](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553628) besides [Purchase/Sale of Business,](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564156) [Purchase/Sale of Investments](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564160) and [Capital Expenditures](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564166). These are the sum of [Investing Activities – Other Sources](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564164) and [Investing Activities – Other Uses](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564165).
