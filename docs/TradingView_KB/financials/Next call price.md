- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Financials - / [ Bonds ](/support/folders/43000594677-bonds/) - / [ Next call price ](/support/solutions/43000737705-next-call-price/) # Next call price The Next call price is the amount, stated as a percentage of the bond's par value, that the issuer must pay if the bond is redeemed (called) at the [next available call date](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000728519/). This price is set in the bond’s terms and conditions and can be equal to, higher, or, in rare cases, lower than the bond's par value. It helps investors assess potential returns or losses if the bond is called early. Previous Previous Next call date Next Next Next put date Launch Supercharts
