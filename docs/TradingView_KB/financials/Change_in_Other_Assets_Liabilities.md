# Change in Other Assets/Liabilities 

#### What is Change in Other Assets/Liabilities?
 Change in Other Assets/Liabilities represents increase or decrease in other assets and liabilities, and other adjustments to working capital not elsewhere classified. This is included in the [Changes in Working Capital](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564147).
