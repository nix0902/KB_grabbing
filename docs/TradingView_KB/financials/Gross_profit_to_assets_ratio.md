# Gross profit to assets ratio 

#### What is Gross profit to asset?
 Gross profit to asset is calculated as [Gross profit](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553611) divided by its average [Total assets](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553623) over a certain period of time.
