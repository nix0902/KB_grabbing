# Income tax, current - domestic 

#### What is Income tax, current - domestic?
 Income tax, current - domestic represents the total amount of tax liability due to the government where the company is domiciled.

For U.K. companies, relief for overseas taxation is netted against the tax expense.

Only annual data are available.

It includes:

- Prior year adjustments of current taxation;
- ACT/ACT written off.
