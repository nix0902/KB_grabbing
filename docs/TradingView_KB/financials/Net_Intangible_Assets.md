# Net Intangible Assets 

#### What are Net Intangible Assets?
 Net Intangible Assets represent the net asset value of all assets that are beneficial to companies, but do not have any physical attributes.

Net Intangible Assets include [Goodwill](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563688).
