Debt to assets ratio counts as [Total debt](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553622) divided by [Total assets](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553623). Formula: Total debt / Total assets What does Debt to asset ratio mean? This indicator is a measure of assets that are growing at the expense of debt. Because of this, you can see how a company acquired its assets over time. It can be used to assess a company's ability to meet its current debt obligations.
