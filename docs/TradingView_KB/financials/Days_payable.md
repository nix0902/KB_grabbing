# Days payable 

#### What is the Days payable?
 Days payable counts as [Accounts payable](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563619) divided by [Cost of goods sold](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553618) and multiplied by the number of days in a period. It is not available for banks.

#### Formula:

Accounts Payable / Cost of goods sold * Days in period

#### What does the Days payable mean?

Companies with high Days payable have the opportunity to have available funds for short-term investments and increase working capital and [free cash flow](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553630). It is worth considering that the high values of Days payable may not always be a positive sign.
