Issuance (Retirement) of Stock, Net represents the net change in the carrying value of common and preferred stock. It is the sum of [Sale of Common & Preferred Stock](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564170) and [Repurchase of Common & Preferred Stock](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564171).
