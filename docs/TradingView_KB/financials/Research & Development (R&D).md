Research & Development represents all direct and indirect costs associated with the creation and development of new processes, applications, and products. Research & Development shows how much money a company is spending on innovation. Why is Research & Development important? Research & Development plays an important role by showing how much money a company is spending to innovate. It can also show how focused a company is on its future, revealing the amount of money spent on new processes, applications, and products. How is Research & Development calculated? It’s calculated by including the following expenses: - Software - Amortization of software - Design and development It excludes: - Customer or government sponsored research - For oil, gas, coal, drilling and mining companies, purchase of mineral rights - Engineering expense - Contributions by government, customers, or other corporations to the company's research and development expenses are excluded if the amount is available.
