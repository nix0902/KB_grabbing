Debt to revenue ratio counts as [Total debt](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553622) divided by [Revenue](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553619). Formula: Total debt / revenue What does Debt to revenue ratio mean? This is a measure that demonstrates how much debt a company has relative to its income. If a company has far more revenue than debt, it means they have more financial freedom and less pressure on the overall business and its operations.
