# Holdings region 

#### **What is Holdings region classification?**
 Regional Funds are investment funds structured around geographic, cultural, and economic similarities of different regions. They offer investors a way to diversify internationally while still focusing on specific areas that might align with their investment goals.

#### **Types of Regional funds:**

Various terms define the scope and focus of regional funds in global investing. These include:

- Global: Encompassing a worldwide investment perspective.
- Emerging markets: Focusing on nations with growing but less developed economies like Brazil, Russia, India, and China.
- Europe, North America, Asia-Pacific, Latin America, Middle East & Africa: Geographically focused funds investing in nations within these respective regions.
- Developed markets: Targeting nations with highly developed economies and advanced technological infrastructure.
- Global Ex-U.S.: Investing globally while excluding the United States market.
- Frontier Markets: Concentrating on smaller, less accessible, yet developing markets.
