Interest Capitalized represents the allowance on borrowed funds used for long-term projects or construction. For utilities, allowance is included for funds used during construction that are related to borrowed funds. It excludes allowance for borrowed funds used during construction that are not interest related. Previous Previous Interest Expense on Debt Next Next Non-Operating Income, excl.
