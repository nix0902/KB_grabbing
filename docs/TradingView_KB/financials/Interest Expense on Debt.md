Interest Expense on Debt represents the service charge for the use of capital before the reduction for interest is capitalized. If interest expense is reported net of interest income, and interest income cannot be found, then the net figure is shown. It includes: - Interest expense on short-term debt; and - Interest expense on long-term debt and capitalized lease obligations.
