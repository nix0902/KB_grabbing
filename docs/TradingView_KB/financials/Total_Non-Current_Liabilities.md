# Total Non-Current Liabilities 

#### What are Total Non-Current Liabilities?
 Total Non-Current Liabilities represent a business’s long-term financial liabilities that are not payable for a year or more.

#### How are Total Non-Current Liabilities calculated?

Total Non-Current Liabilities are the sum of the following:

- [Long Term Debt](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553621);
- [Provision for Risks & Charge](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563535);
- [Deferred Tax Liabilities](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563536);
- [Deferred Income, Non-Current](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563540); and
- [Other Non-Current Liabilities, Total](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563545).
