Interest coverage counts as [Operating income](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563464) divided by [Interest expense](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563466). Formula: Operating income / Interest expense What does Interest coverage mean? It measures the stock that a company has to pay interest on its debt.
