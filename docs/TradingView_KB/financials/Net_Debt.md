# Net Debt 

#### What is Net Debt?
 Net Debt represents the amount of debt that would remain after a company had paid off as much debt as possible with its liquid assets. This financial metric shows how well a company can handle its current obligations and if it has the ability to take on more debt in the future.

Net Debt can be calculated as [Total Debt](https://www.tradingview.com/chart/?solution=43000553622) minus [Cash & Short Term Investments](https://www.tradingview.com/chart/?solution=43000563702).
