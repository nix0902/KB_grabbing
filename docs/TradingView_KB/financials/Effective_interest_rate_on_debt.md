# Effective interest rate on debt 

#### What is the Effective interest rate on debt?
 This metric shows the ratio that is paid by the borrower on debt. Effective interest rate on debt counts as [Interest expense](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563466) divided by the average of [Total debt](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553622) over the last two periods.

#### Formula:

Interest expense / Average debt
