Shareholders' Equity represents the value of shareholders' equity, belonging to both Preferred and Common Equity Shareholders. How is Shareholders' Equity calculated? Shareholders' Equity is the sum of [Common Equity, Total](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563866) and [Preferred Stock, Carrying Value](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563879).
