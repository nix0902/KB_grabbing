# Front month 

#### What is *Front month*?
 In futures trading, the term * front month* refers to the front or nearest expiration month contract. It rolls over to the next contract when the current front-month contract expires. This series is widely used for short-term analysis and trading, as it accurately reflects the current market sentiment and price movements.
