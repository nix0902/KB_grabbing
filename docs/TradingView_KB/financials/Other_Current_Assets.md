# Other Current Assets 

#### What are Other Current Assets?
 Other Current Assets represent [Current Assets](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000557441) besides [Cash & Short Term Investments](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563702), [Total Receivables, Net](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563738), [Inventories](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563745) and [Prepaid Expenses](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563757).
