# Purchase/Sale of Business, Net 

#### What is Purchase/Sale of Business, Net?
 Purchase/Sale of Business, Net represents the amount of cash that was used in operations related to the purchase or sale of businesses or subsidiaries.

It is the sum of [Sale of Fixed Assets & Businesses](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564158) and the [Purchase/Acquisition of Business](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000564159).
