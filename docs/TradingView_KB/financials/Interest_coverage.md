# Interest coverage 

#### What is Interest coverage?
 Interest coverage counts as [Operating income](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563464) divided by [Interest expense](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563466).

#### Formula:

Operating income / Interest expense

#### What does Interest coverage mean?

It measures the stock that a company has to pay interest on its debt. It is used to determine how easy it is for a company to pay its interest expense on outstanding debt.
