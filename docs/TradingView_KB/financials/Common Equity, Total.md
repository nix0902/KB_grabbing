Common Equity, Total represents the invested capital, accumulated retained earnings, surpluses and reserves, attributable to holders of a parent company's common stock, where net of ownership interest is reacquired by the company. How is Common Equity, Total calculated? Common Equity, Total is the sum of [Retained Earnings](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563867), [Paid in Capital](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563871) and [Other Common Equity](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563877).
