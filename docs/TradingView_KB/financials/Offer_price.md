# Offer price 

#### What is the Offer price?
 The offer price is the final price per share set for the IPO.

It is the price at which investors purchase shares during the offering. This price is determined right before trading begins, after the order book is finalized. It also serves as a reference for the stock’s initial market value.

#### What is the Offer price range?

The offer price range is the estimated interval for the offer price that the company and underwriters publish before the IPO.

It represents the expected valuation, the initial target pricing for investors, and a guideline used during the book-building process.

The range may be adjusted before the final price is set.
