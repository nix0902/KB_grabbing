# Net Income Before Discontinued Operations 

#### What is Net Income Before Discontinued Operations?
 Net Income Before Discontinued Operations is the amount of money a company earns after all operating expenses, interest, taxes and dividends on preferred shares have been paid for ([Net Income](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000553617)), but before [Discontinued Operations](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000563502).
