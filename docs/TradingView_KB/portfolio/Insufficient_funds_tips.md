# Insufficient funds tips 
 When the system detects insufficient funds at the time of an asset purchase, you will see a notification prompting you to add a cash transaction.

 Click Add Funds, verify the date and amount, and then click Save.

Note: The insufficient funds check is performed specifically for the date of the trade transaction. Even if you have added funds later and your current balance is positive, the notification will still appear.
