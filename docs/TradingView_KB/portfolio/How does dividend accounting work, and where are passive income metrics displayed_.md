Dividends are not currently included in the calculation of portfolio metrics, and passive income indicators are neither calculated nor displayed. We are actively working on expanding the platform’s functionality. In upcoming updates, we plan to introduce: - automatic accounting of dividends received from assets in the portfolio; - display of passive income metrics, both aggregated and per individual instrument. At the moment, information about expected dividend payments is available under the Overview → [Upcoming dividends](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000756104/) tab. This data is provided for informational purposes — to give an estimate of upcoming payments. If you have comments or suggestions on this topic, please contact support — this will help us better align future updates with your priorities.
