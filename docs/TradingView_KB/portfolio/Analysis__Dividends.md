# Analysis: Dividends 
 This chart shows the monthly dividend income for your portfolio. Each bar represents the total dividends received in a given month.

You can view the data for the trailing twelve months (TTM) or select a specific year from the options below the chart.

- Monthly Dividend Income — the total dividend amount received in the selected month.

- Use the year tabs (TTM, 2025, 2024, 2023, etc.) to quickly navigate between different periods.

This visualization allows you to track dividend trends over time and helps you analyze the seasonality and consistency of your dividend income.
