# Why did my Cash become negative? 
 This may happen for the following reasons:

- You made a transaction on an asset without having sufficient free cash available. Please note that the commission is also deducted from the Cash value when a transaction is executed. More details in the following article: [Portfolio Summary: Portfolio Value and Cash](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000756016/)
- [You created a portfolio from a watchlist](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000756009/) and did not add a deposit.
- You performed a Withdrawal or a Taxes and fees operation without sufficient free cash.

In such cases, the system will automatically detect a negative balance and suggest compensating it through a Deposit transaction.

More about transaction types and how to add them: [Types of transactions and how to add them](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000756013/)

#### What if none of the above helped? 

If your free cash became negative for another reason, please contact support.
