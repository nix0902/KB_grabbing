# Why are some dates in the calendar disabled? 
 Some dates in the calendar are unavailable for selection because markets are closed on those days. These include:

- weekends (e.g., Saturday and Sunday)
- official public holidays when no trading occurs and yield data is not updated

Such dates are disabled in the calendar to ensure only relevant and accurate information is shown.
