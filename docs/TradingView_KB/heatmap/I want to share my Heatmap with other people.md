- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / Heatmap - / [ How to share a Heatmap ](/support/folders/43000591082-how-to-share-a-heatmap/) - / [ I want to share my Heatmap with other people ](/support/solutions/43000707286-i-want-to-share-my-heatmap-with-other-people/) # I want to share my Heatmap with other people You have five ways to share the Heatmap: - Download image - Copy image - Copy link - Share on X.com - Share on Facebook The **Download image** action saves the current state of the Heatmap as an image: The **Copy Image** action is equivalent to the previous Download image action. However, the image is not saved as a file in this case — it is copied to the clipboard. You can paste the image into a document or social media platform if needed. The **Copy link** action copies the current link of the Heatmap you select. Please note that only the following parameters are passed in the link: - Selected data source; - Selected color and size parameters; - Symbol grouping (Grouping by Sector or no grouping). The **Share on X.com** or **Share on Facebook** action sends a link with a preview of your Heatmap to your wall with your chosen options.
