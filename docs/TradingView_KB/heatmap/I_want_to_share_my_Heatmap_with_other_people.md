# I want to share my Heatmap with other people 
 You have five ways to share the Heatmap:

- Download image

- Copy image

- Copy link

- Share on X.com

- Share on Facebook

The **Download image** action saves the current state of the Heatmap as an image:

The **Copy Image** action is equivalent to the previous Download image action. However, the image is not saved as a file in this case — it is copied to the clipboard. You can paste the image into a document or social media platform if needed.

The **Copy link** action copies the current link of the Heatmap you select.

Please note that only the following parameters are passed in the link:

- Selected data source;
- Selected color and size parameters;
- Symbol grouping (Grouping by Sector or no grouping).

The **Share on X.com** or **Share on Facebook** action sends a link with a preview of your Heatmap to your wall with your chosen options.
