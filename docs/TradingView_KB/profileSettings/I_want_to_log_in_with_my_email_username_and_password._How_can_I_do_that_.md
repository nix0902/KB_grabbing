# I want to log in with my email/username and password. How can I do that? 
 If you’ve lost access to your social network (including Gmail, Apple ID, Yahoo, etc.) or if you would prefer to log in to TradingView using your username/password, just follow these simple steps:

- Open your [Account Settings](https://www.tradingview.com/settings/#account-settings) and set an email and password for your TradingView account (if you have not already done so, or if you can’t remember them): 
- In the same tab remove the social network in the "Linked external accounts" section: 

If your social network account has been compromised or hacked, we strongly advise that you change the email address and unlink the network from your TradingView account in your profile settings. That will make sure that the hacker cannot access your TradingView account. 

We also suggest that you enable [2-factor authentication](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000572460/) to make your TradingView account even more secure.
