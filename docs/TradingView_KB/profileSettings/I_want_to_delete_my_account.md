# I want to delete my account 
 To delete your account, you need to go to Settings and billing - [Account settings](https://www.tradingview.com/settings/#account-settings) and click D*elete account*:

Your account will be deleted after 30 days. During this time, you can stop the deletion process at any time and continue using TradingView.

Please keep in mind that according to our [Terms of Use](https://www.tradingview.com/policies/), some data, such as published ideas/scripts and messages sent to other users, will be saved as they have already integrated into our system. This data must be stored to maintain the integrity of the platform, as well as for legitimate business processes, including audits and security purposes. When your account is deleted, we ask search engines and other third-party apps to comply with GDPR and remove your data. You can reach out to search engines separately to expedite the removal.
