It copies your price bars, creates a drawing out of them and lets you drag it around the chart. You can, for example, compare recent price movements to past or future moves: Style In the Style property dialog, it is possible to change the appearance of a Bars pattern drawn: Color Sets bars pattern sticks color and opacity level. Mirrored/Flipped Use these checkboxes to reflect a bar pattern vertically or horizontally. Mode Sets the shape and price source for the pattern drawn. This pattern may be displayed in the form of bar sticks as well as in the form of line with different price sources for its points: Visibility In the Visibility properties dialog, you can switch the Bars patern displaying on charts of different timeframes: Previous Previous Comment Next Next Brush Launch Supercharts

### **Top Bar & Ticker Info**  
- **Left**: “Apple Inc · 1D · NASDAQ” (ticker, timeframe, exchange).  
- **Right**: Price data: *O229.22 H234.51 L229.02 C234.07 +4.04 (+1.76%)* (open, high, low, close, change, % change).  
- **Timeframes**: 1s, 1m, 5m, 15m, 30m, 1h, 4h, D (1D), W, M (dropdown for customization).  
- **Tools**: Chart type (candlestick), indicators, layout, full-screen, and “Publish” button.  


### **Chart Area**  
- **Candlestick Chart**: Shows price action over months (Sep–Feb). Green candles = price increase; red = decrease.  
- **Y-Axis (Right)**: Price scale (USD) from ~217.50 to 260.00.  
- **X-Axis (Bottom)**: Date range (Sep 2024–Feb 2025) with key dates (e.g., *Wed 20 Nov ’24*, *Wed 18 Dec ’24*).  


### **“Bars Pattern” Modal (Style Settings)**  
A pop-up for customizing candlestick/bar styles:  
- **Tabs**: *Style* (active) and *Visibility*.  
- **Color**: Blue square (select bar color).  
- **Mode**: Dropdown set to *“HL bars”* (high-low bars).  
- **Checkboxes**: *Mirrored* (reflect bars) and *Flipped* (invert direction).  
- **Buttons**: *Template* (dropdown), *Cancel*, *Ok* (apply changes).  


### **Left Sidebar (Tools)**  
Icons for:  
- Zoom, drawing (lines, shapes), Fibonacci, text, indicators, and other charting tools.  


### **Bottom Bar**  
- **Timeframe Buttons**: 1D, 5D, 1M, 3M, 6M, YTD, 1Y, 5Y, *All* (select chart period).  
- **Date Picker**: Calendar icon (adjust date range).  
- **Timestamp**: *11:59:10 UTC* (current time).  
- **Tabs**: *Pine Editor* (scripting), *Strategy Tester*, *Replay Trading*, *Trading Panel*.  


### **Right Sidebar**  
- **Price Scale**: Repeats y-axis values.  
- **Icons**: Notes, clock (time), alerts, chat, and other utilities.  


### **Key Purpose**  
The interface is for **technical analysis**—traders use it to visualize price trends, customize chart styles, and access tools for analysis/execution. The “Bars Pattern” modal lets users tailor how price bars (candles) appear on the chart.
