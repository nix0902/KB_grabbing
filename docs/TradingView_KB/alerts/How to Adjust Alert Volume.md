---

- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Alerts - / [ Alerts settings ](/support/folders/43000547663-alerts-settings/) - / [ How to Adjust Alert Volume ](/support/solutions/43000740625-how-to-adjust-alert-volume/) # How to Adjust Alert Volume The **Alert Volume** setting can be found in the **Settings → Alerts**. It works globally, applying to all alerts at once. 1. Open the** Settings** via the **Alerts Menu** in the right toolbar. 2. Navigate to the **Alerts** section. Use the **Alert Volume** slider to increase or decrease the volume to your preferred level. Please note: This setting is available on the website and not in the mobile or desktop apps.
