# How to receive phone notifications for alerts

---

- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / Alerts - / [ Alerts notifications ](/support/folders/43000547712-alerts-notifications/) - / [ How to receive phone notifications for alerts ](/support/solutions/43000474389-how-to-receive-phone-notifications-for-alerts/) # How to receive phone notifications for alerts To get notified about an alert on your phone, please follow the instructions: - Download the latest TradingView app from [App Store](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/tradingview-trading-community-charts-and-quotes/id1205990992) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.tradingview.tradingviewapp) - Log into your TradingView account in the app - Allow the TradingView app to send push notifications. For Android devices: open TradingView app settings and enable push notifications. For iOS devices: go to Settings -&gt; Notifications - select TradingView app - turn on Allow Notifications. - Check the Notify in App box when creating an alert.
