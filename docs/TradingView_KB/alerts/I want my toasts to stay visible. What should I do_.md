What should I do? ](/support/solutions/43000737982-i-want-my-toasts-to-stay-visible-what-should-i-do/) # I want my toasts to stay visible. What should I do? By default, the "Automatically hide toasts" setting is enabled, causing toasts to close after 20 seconds. If you prefer to keep them visible until closed manually: 1. Open Alerts settings via the Options menu. 2. Uncheck the "Automatically hide toasts" box.
