To set up unexpired alerts, follow these steps: 1. Open the Create/Edit alert window; 2. Click on Expiration section's button; 3. Enable the Open-ended option in appeared pop-up and then click on 'Set unexpired'; 4. Save your alert. NOTE! - Open-ended alerts are available for Premium and Ultimate plans only. - The Open-ended feature is not available for alerts created on watchlists. - Alerts are automatically deactivated if all of the following conditions are met: 1. The alert was created more than one year ago; 2. The alert hasn't triggered for over a year: 3. The alert hasn't been edited for over a year.
