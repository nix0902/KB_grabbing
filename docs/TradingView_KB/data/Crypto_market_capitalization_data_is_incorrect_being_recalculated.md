# Crypto market capitalization data is incorrect/being recalculated 
 [These symbols](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000550480) can return different values based on whether historical or real-time data was used in the calculation. Some of the symbols are calculated using the 125 most popular cryptocurrency pairs. Therefore, at different moments in time, this list may contain different symbols. As a result, the data calculated for the CRYPTOCAP symbols may change over time. Every time you refresh the chart page, you may see slightly different bars. Most of these symbols are [spreads](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000502298) and often change for their own reasons.
