# Macro Maps on TradingView: explore, compare, track 
 Understanding global trends and the current market state is the foundation of financial analysis. Whatever asset you trade, knowledge of key economic indicators can help you enhance your trading strategy. With our Macro Maps, you can identify global trends to navigate the complex and ever-changing financial landscape more effectively.

#### What are Macro Maps 

Macro Maps visualize global economic indicators by color-coding each country on the world map according to their economic performance. You can compare economies using various indicators including GDP, labor statistics, price levels, and many others.

Additionally, you can adjust them to display data for a particular time period with precision up to a month, allowing for detailed historical analysis and trend identification.

#### How to access Macro Maps 

To start your financial analysis, click on the "Products" button on the right toolbar and select "Macro Maps."

They will appear in the Overlay sheet. If you're using [Supercharts](https://www.tradingview.com/chart/), consider [splitting your screen](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000753069-simple-steps-to-split-view-mode/) to simultaneously view the asset and understand the underlying factors driving its movement.

#### How to use Macro Maps 

This tool is remarkably straightforward to use. The comprehensive data it displays can lay the foundation for your next investment decisions. This is because broad economic conditions affect individual economies, and none of them exist in isolation – understanding these interconnections is crucial for successful trading.

First, you need to pick the indicators you want to start your analysis with. In the top left, click on the magnifying glass icon to access all available indicators. Or simply choose from those already displayed at the top of the maps.

Marking indicators as favorites helps you quickly switch between them and configure the Maps according to your analysis.

To change the data, simply drag the scrubber at the bottom. Below it, you'll see the criteria for color-coding countries according to specific values. These criteria will automatically adjust whenever you change the time period.

#### Oh, and one more global economy thing… 

If you need more diverse data on the [world economy](https://www.tradingview.com/markets/world-economy/), our market pages provide access to economic indicators heatmaps, TradingViewers' top stories, latest market news, and other valuable information that can help you understand markets from multiple perspectives and make more informed trading decisions.
