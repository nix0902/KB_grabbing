# How to purchase additional market data 

Most of the stock and futures exchanges we support charge fees for their real-time data. Currently, our paid plans don't cover those fees even if you have an active TradingView subscription.

If you need a paid real-time data subscription, please note that we have data packages designed for professional and non-professional subscribers. The rate and availability of a particular data package depend on your status and the exchange regulations. Note that professional users must purchase our professional market data subscriptions. For more information on who qualifies as a professional subscriber, please refer to this [related article](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000677382-what-is-the-difference-between-professional-and-non-professional-subscriptions/). 

Not all our data is delayed. For example, all the cryptocurrency exchanges presented on TradingView provide real-time data.

**! Note:** By default, the U.S. stock market data is provided by [Cboe Exchange](https://www.cboe.com/us/equities/market_statistics/). Thus it's slightly different from that of the primary exchanges. To get real-time data directly from NASDAQ, NYSE, or ARCA, you can add it to your plan.

If you're unsure which data subscription you need to purchase for a specific chart, there are two ways to find out.

1. Click on the chart's market status; the relevant subscription will appear in the dropdown menu.

2. Enter the ticker into the "Symbol search" field; the exchange that lists this particular instrument will appear next to the ticker.

Also, you can purchase real-time data through your account:

- Click on your avatar
- Choose "Settings and billing"
- Click "Subscriptions" in the "Billing" section
- Click "Explore now" in the "Real-time market data" section
- See the active subscriptions or click "Explore now"

Alternatively, you can do it from this article by navigating to the [Market Data](https://www.tradingview.com/data-coverage/) page.

On the Supercharts, if you see the "Data is delayed" message in the chart's status line, click on the "Learn more" button to purchase real-time data.

### **Top Navigation Bar**
- **Left Side**: 
  - `KC1! Coffee C Futures · 1D · ICEUS`: Ticker symbol, timeframe (1D = 1 day), and exchange (ICEUS).
  - `O286.90 H299.20 L286.85 C298.30 +11.80 (+4.12%)`: Key price data (Open, High, Low, Close, Change, % Change).
  - `298.30 SELL` (red box): Current **sell price** (red = ask/bid spread).
  - `298.35 BUY` (blue box): Current **buy price** (blue = bid/ask spread).
  - `0.05`: Bid-ask spread (difference between buy/sell prices).
  - `Vol 14.09K`: Current **volume** (14,090 contracts).
  - `^` (up arrow): Collapsible menu for additional market data.

- **Middle Icons** (from left to right):
  - `+`: Add new chart/indicator.
  - `D`: Toggle timeframe (e.g., 1D, 5D, 1M).
  - `⚖️`: Balance/symbol info.
  - `📈 Indicators`: Add technical indicators (e.g., moving averages, RSI).
  - `📊`: Chart type selector (candlestick, line, bar).
  - `⏰ Alert`: Set price alerts.
  - `⏮️ Replay`: Replay market data for backtesting.
  - `↩️/↪️`: Undo/redo actions.

- **Right Side**:
  - `TradingView` (dropdown): Switch accounts or access settings.
  - `Save`: Save the chart layout.
  - `🔄`: Refresh data.
  - `⚙️`: Chart settings.
  - `[]`: Full-screen mode.
  - `📷`: Screenshot tool.
  - `Publish`: Share the chart publicly.
