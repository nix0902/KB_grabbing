# Why does TradingView show USD and BTC pairs that are not listed on the exchange? 
 We independently calculate the missing prices of coins in USD and BTC for some popular exchanges (Binance, Bitfinex, and a few more, the list may change), if the exchange itself does not provide such data. Such symbols are synthetic and can be calculated by using the rate from other exchanges.
