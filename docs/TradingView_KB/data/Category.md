# Category 
 The category of an economic indicator describes the specific aspect of economic or social activity it represents. It serves as a classification system, grouping indicators based on their thematic focus. Categories provide a framework for understanding the nature of the measured economic variable. Examples include "Labor", "Prices", "Government", "Business", etc.
