# The price on my chart differs from the price in the watchlist 
 If your chart is set to Heikin Ashi, the price shown on the chart might differ from the price that is displayed in the watch list. This is due to the Heikin Ashi formula, as in this case the price is not the same as on ordinary candles chart.
 If you want to see only real price updates, please, open the chart settings menu, go to the Symbol tab and check the *Show real prices**...* option.
