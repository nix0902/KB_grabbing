# Economic Optimism Index (EOI) 
 Assessments and expectations of consumers and entrepreneurs regarding the economic situation.

All countries for which *EOI* is available are listed below:

- Albania
- Brazil
- Euro Area
- Faroe Islands
- Israel
- Latvia
- Slovakia
- South Korea
- Turkey
- United States
