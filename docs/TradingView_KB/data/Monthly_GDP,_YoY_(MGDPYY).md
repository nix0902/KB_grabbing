# Monthly GDP, YoY (MGDPYY) 
 Measure of the annualized change in the inflation-adjusted value of all goods and services produced by the economy.

All countries for which *MGDPYY* is available are listed below:

- Argentina
- Azerbaijan
- Belarus
- Finland
- Georgia
- Kazakhstan
- Kyrgyzstan
- Mexico
- Peru
- Russia
- United Kingdom
