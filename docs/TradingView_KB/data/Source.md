# Source 
 The source of an economic indicator refers to the origin or agency responsible for collecting and disseminating the relevant data. It identifies the authoritative body or institution that produces and publishes the statistics. Common sources include national statistical agencies, central banks, and government departments. Understanding the source is crucial for assessing the reliability and credibility of the data.
