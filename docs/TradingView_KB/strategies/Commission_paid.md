# Commission paid 
 The total cumulative cost of transaction fees charged by the broker or exchange for executing trades. This metric reflects the direct overhead of your trading activity.

**Exclusions**: This value strictly represents documented fees and does not include slippage (the difference between the expected price and the actual execution price).

**Note**: This amount is already subtracted from the Net P&L to provide a true reflection of your "take-home" profit.

You can also find more information about commission [here](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000628599/).
