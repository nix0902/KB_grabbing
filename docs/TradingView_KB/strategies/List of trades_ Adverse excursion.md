- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / [ List of trades: Adverse excursion ](/support/solutions/43000681750-list-of-trades-adverse-excursion/) # List of trades: Adverse excursion The adverse excursion of the trade according to the strategy, as well as the maximum percentage loss. Let's look how its value is calculated: In this trade, we entered a long position on Bitcoin (BTC/USD) at a price of $105,585.2, and exited the trade at a price of $107,613.3. While the position was open, the lowest price reached was $102,756.0. **The ****adverse excursion is calculated as follows:** $105,585.2 (Entry Price) - $102,756.0 (Lowest Price) = $2,829.2 **The ****percentage value is calculated as:** (2,829.2 (Drawdown) / 105,585.2 (Entry Price)) × 100% ≈ 2.68% You can find the adverse excursion value in the Strategy Tester in two places in the Adverse excursion column on the [List of trades](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000681737/) tab Previous Previous List of trades: Favorable excursion Next Next Initial capital Launch Supercharts
