# How Deep Backtesting works 
 Deep Backtesting allows you to test strategies using all available historical data for a selected symbol, rather than being limited to the data currently loaded on the chart.

This feature is available for Premium and higher plan users. The limit of available historical data is up to two million bars and one million trades.

To use it, open a strategy and select the time range you want to test. Deep Backtesting will launch automatically — you'll recognize it by the pink icon indicator.

! Note: Deep Backtesting results are only shown in the Strategy Tester tab and are not displayed on the chart.

When you modify the strategy, click Update report to refresh the information.

To stop Deep Backtesting, click Reset to chart session.
