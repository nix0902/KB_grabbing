It expresses the Largest Winning Trade (Monetary) as a percentage of your Gross Profit. **Exclusions**: This only looks at the relationship between your single best winner and your total winners; it does not factor in your losing trades. **Strategic value**: - High percentage (e.g., &gt;30%): Suggests "Fragility." Your strategy may be heavily dependent on a single "star trade." If you remove that one trade, the strategy might become unprofitable. - Low percentage (e.g., &lt;10%): Suggests "Robustness." Your gains are distributed across many trades, meaning your edge is consistent and less reliant on rare market "lottery" wins.
