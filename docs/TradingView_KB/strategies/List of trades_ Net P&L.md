Let's look at how the value for the Profit column is calculated: In this example, we bought 1 share of AAPL at the opening on June 15 and sold it at the opening of June 22. The initial capital was $1000. We bought a stock at $333.25 and sold for $351.34, i.e. profit amounted to (351.34-333.25) = $18.09, or as a percentage (18.09/(333.25*1))*100% = 5.43%.
