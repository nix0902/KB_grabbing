Shows how much, on average, the capital increases during growth periods. Calculation: - Identify all equity growth periods - Calculate the run-up for each period: - Average the run-ups: Notes: - If a run-up is less than 1 unit, display &lt;1 or No Data. - Uses close-to-close equity.
