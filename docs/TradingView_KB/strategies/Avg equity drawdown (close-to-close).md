### Key Elements:  
- **Formula Components**:  
  - *Drawdownᵢ*: The drawdown (loss from a peak) for the *i*-th period.  
  - *Equity Peakᵢ*: The highest equity value reached during the *i*-th period.  
  - *Equity Troughᵢ*: The lowest equity value (trough) following the *i*-th peak.  
- **Purpose**: Quantifies the maximum loss from a peak to a subsequent trough, a critical risk metric in trading to assess strategy volatility.
