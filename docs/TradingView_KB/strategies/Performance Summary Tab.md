### Purpose of Elements  
- **Navigation Tabs**: Switch between strategy testing views (e.g., `Overview` for high-level stats, `List of Trades` for individual trades).  
- **Strategy Controls**: Adjust strategy settings, add/remove strategies, or import/export.  
- **Deep Backtesting Toggle**: Enables more rigorous (slower) backtesting for deeper analysis.  
- **Performance Metrics**: Quantify strategy profitability, risk, and efficiency (e.g., `Net Profit` shows overall gain/loss; `Sharpe/Sortino Ratios` measure risk-adjusted returns; `Max Drawdown` highlights peak loss).  


This interface helps traders evaluate a strategy’s historical performance, risk, and profitability across all trades, long trades, and short trades.
