- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / [ List of trades: Favorable excursion ](/support/solutions/43000681748-list-of-trades-favorable-excursion/) # List of trades: Favorable excursion The favorable excursion of the trade according to the strategy, as well as the maximum percentage gain. Let's look how its value is calculated: In this trade, we entered a long position on Bitcoin (BTC/USD) at a price of $105,585.2, and exited the trade at a price of $107,613.3. While the position was open, the highest price reached was $110,719.0. **The ****Run-up is calculated as follows:** $110,719.0 (Highest Price) - $105,585.2 (Entry Price) = $5,133.8 **The ****percentage value is calculated as:** (5,133.8 (Run-up) / 105,585.2 (Entry Price)) × 100% ≈ 4.86% You can find the favorable excursion value in the Strategy Tester in the Favorable excursion column on the [List of trades](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000681737/) tab Previous Previous List of trades: Cumulative P&L Next Next List of trades: Adverse excursion Launch Supercharts
