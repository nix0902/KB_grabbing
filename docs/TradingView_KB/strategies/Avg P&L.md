- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Strategies - / [ Strategy report metrics ](/support/folders/43000599093-strategy-report-metrics/) - / [ Avg P&L ](/support/solutions/43000681715-avg-p-l/) # Avg P&L The mean financial result generated by each closed position within the strategy. This metric tells, on average, how much money each trade contributes to (or subtracts from) the account balance. **Exclusions**: This calculation is based strictly on realized results and does not account for the floating profits or losses of currently open positions. **Strategic value**: This is a vital "viability" metric. For a strategy to be sustainable in live markets, the Avg P&L must remain significantly higher than combined commission paid and estimated slippage. If the Avg P&L is too "thin," the strategy risks being "eaten alive" by transaction costs. Calculation Formula The average is determined by dividing the Net P&L by the total volume of completed trades.
