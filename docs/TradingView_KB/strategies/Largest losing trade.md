# Largest losing trade

- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Strategies - / [ Strategy report metrics ](/support/folders/43000599093-strategy-report-metrics/) - / [ Largest losing trade ](/support/solutions/43000681722-largest-losing-trade/) # Largest losing trade The single greatest realized loss incurred by a closed position during the testing period. This metric identifies the absolute "worst-case scenario" for a single trade in the strategy's history. **Exclusions**: This metric strictly considers closed positions. It does not account for floating losses in currently open trades, even if they are currently deeper than the historical maximum. **Strategic value**: If the Largest losing trade is significantly larger than your Avg losing trade, it may indicate a market "gap" event, or an issue with position sizing (taking too much risk on one trade).
