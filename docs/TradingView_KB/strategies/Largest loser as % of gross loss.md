- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Strategies - / [ Strategy report metrics ](/support/folders/43000599093-strategy-report-metrics/) - / [ Largest loser as % of gross loss ](/support/solutions/43000772910-largest-loser-as-of-gross-loss/) # Largest loser as % of gross loss This metric identifies your single most significant monetary loss as a percentage of your total accumulated losses. It measures the "concentration of risk" within your losing trades. **Exclusions**: This calculation compares your single worst loss to the Gross Loss (the sum of all losing trades). It does not factor in your winning trades or net profit. **Strategic value**: - High percentage (e.g., &gt;30%): Indicates a "Fat Tail" risk. A single failure is responsible for a large chunk of your total losses, often suggesting that a stop-loss was missed, a market gap occurred, or position sizing was too aggressive. - Low percentage (e.g., &lt;10%): Indicates "Controlled Risk." Your losses are small and evenly distributed, which is a hallmark of a disciplined strategy with effective risk management.
