- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Strategies - / [ Strategy report metrics ](/support/folders/43000599093-strategy-report-metrics/) - / [ Net P&L ](/support/solutions/43000681684-net-p-l/) # Net P&L The cumulative realized profit or loss achieved by the strategy over the selected period. This represents the "bottom line" of all finalized trades. **Realized gains only**: This metric does not include profits or losses from currently open positions. It only accounts for trades that have been fully closed and settled. **Calculation**: The mathematical sum of all closed trades in the Net P&L column (found in the List of Trades tab).
