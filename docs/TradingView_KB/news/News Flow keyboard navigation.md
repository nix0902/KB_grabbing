- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / News - / [ News Flow ](/support/folders/43000594706-news-flow/) - / [ News Flow keyboard navigation ](/support/solutions/43000728826-news-flow-keyboard-navigation/) # News Flow keyboard navigation News Flow supports keyboard navigation. Navigation by news headlines - Down arrow - highlights the first news in the list. - Enter - opens news in the reading area. - Up/down arrows, PgUp/PgDn, Home/End, space - move through the news headline table. If the reading area is open, the content of the news changes as you move around the table. Switching focus - Tab or right arrow - switch focus from the news headline table to the full news. - Shift+Tab or left arrow - switch focus from full news to news headline table. Navigation through the full text of the news if the news is open for reading - Up/down arrows, PgUp/PgDn, Home/End, space - move through the full text of the news. - Esc - closes the full text of the news.
