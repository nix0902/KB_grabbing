# How to sign out of your account in Desktop app 
 Before signing out, please ensure you have saved all changes to your chart layouts.

- Open any tab containing a chart
- Click on your profile icon in the upper left corner
- To sign out, simply click on the "Sign Out" option in the opened drop-down menu
