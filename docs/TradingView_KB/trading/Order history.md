**Описание:** This TradingView image displays the **History tab** of a trading platform, showing a detailed order history for Apple (NASDAQ:AAPL) trades. Here’s a breakdown of all elements:  


### **Top Navigation & Filters**  
- **Tabs**: *Active (1)*, *Orders (6)*, *History* (selected, underlined), *Account History*, *Trading Journal* (for switching between sections).  
- **Order Status Filters**: *All (100)*, *Filled (54)*, *Cancelled (41)*, *Rejected (5)* (to filter orders by status).  


### **Table Columns (Order Details)**  
Each row represents a single trade, with columns:  
- **Symbol**: Apple logo + “NASDAQ:AAPL” (identifies the asset).  
- **Side**: *Sell* (red) or *Buy* (blue) (direction of the trade).  
- **Type**: Order type (e.g., *Stop*, *Market*, *Limit*).  
- **Qty**: Quantity (all trades here are 1 unit).  
- **Limit Price**: Price for limit orders (blank for market/stop orders).  
- **Stop Price**: Trigger price for stop orders (blank for market/limit orders).  
- **Fill Price**: Price at which the order executed (blank for cancelled/rejected orders).  
- **Status**: *Filled* (green, executed), *Cancelled* (orange, not executed), *Rejected* (red, invalid order).  
- **Commission**: Blank (no fees shown).  
- **Leverage**: *1:1* (no leverage used).  
- **Margin**: Margin required (matches fill/stop price for filled orders; blank for cancelled/rejected).  


### **Sample Orders (Key Rows)**  
- **Filled Stop Sell**: Sell 1 AAPL at stop price $228.78, filled at $228.79.  
- **Filled Market Buy**: Buy 1 AAPL at market price $228.90.  
- **Cancelled Stop Sell**: Sell 1 AAPL at stop $229.11 (order cancelled, no fill price).  
- **Rejected Market Sell**: Sell 1 AAPL (order rejected, no fill price).  


### **Purpose**  
This view helps traders review past trades, analyze execution prices, identify patterns (e.g., successful vs. cancelled orders), and track performance. The filters and columns enable quick sorting/analysis of order history.
