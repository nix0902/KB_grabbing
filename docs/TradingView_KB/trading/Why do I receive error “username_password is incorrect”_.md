AMP users can access several trading platforms. Our integration with AMP is implemented via CQG. To log into your AMP account on TradingView, you need to use CQG-powered credentials, that will also work here: [https://m.cqg.com/cqg/desktop/main](https://m.cqg.com/cqg/desktop/main%C2%A0).
