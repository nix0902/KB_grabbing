- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / [ What instruments can I trade through City Index? ](/support/solutions/43000676571-what-instruments-can-i-trade-through-city-index/) # What instruments can I trade through City Index? To find all tradable symbols for City Index, connect to the broker via the Trading Panel, then open the Symbol Search and, finally, make sure that the City Index checkbox is active. This feature is only available once you’ve logged into your brokerage account. Then, if you are looking for a specific symbol, simply use the search bar. Please note, spread bet trading is **not yet** available via City Index at TradingView. Previous Previous Why do I receive the “Failed to login: Authorization failed” error?
