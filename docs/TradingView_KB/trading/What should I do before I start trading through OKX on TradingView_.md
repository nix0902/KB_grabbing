Step 1. Once you have an OKX account, navigate to the Trade panel in the top left corner and choose either Basic trading or Margin trading. Step 2. Now click on the gear in the top right corner of the screen to get to the settings menu Step 3. Click on the Account mode button and select any mode except for Portfolio Margin Congratulations!
