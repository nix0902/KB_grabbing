Our charts use price quotes that cover a wider time range than the actual trading hours of the instrument. To display data outside of the active trading session on TradingView, right-click on the chart and select "Settings". On the "Symbol" tab, navigate to "Session", and select "Extended Trading Hours" from the dropdown menu. Previous Previous How can I generate an account statement?
