If you have a live broker account and you have already **purchased real-time data** with your broker, you do not need to pay for them again in order to get access on TradingView. Just verify your account and its available subscriptions to get real-time data on our website. Keep in mind that data on free plan updates every few seconds, while any paid plan provides faster updates if trades are happening. To do this, you need to connect to your broker in the Trading Panel .
