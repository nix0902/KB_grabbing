- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / [ Which Tickmill trading accounts can I connect to TradingView? ](/support/solutions/43000765948-which-tickmill-trading-accounts-can-i-connect-to-tradingview/) # Which Tickmill trading accounts can I connect to TradingView? TradingView is available for **Tickmill Trader Raw** accounts, with the option to use your demo account for risk-free trading. Please note that we’re currently unable to provide TradingView access to our clients based in the EU and the UK. Learn more about Tickmill trading account types on the Tickmill's website ([Accounts Overview](https://www.tickmill.com/trading/accounts-overview)).
