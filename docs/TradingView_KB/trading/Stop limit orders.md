- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Trading - / [ Trading basics ](/support/folders/43000597927-trading-basics/) - / [ Stop limit orders ](/support/solutions/43000754945-stop-limit-orders/) # Stop limit orders A stop limit order gives you precision and control. Like a stop order, it activates when a trigger price is reached. But instead of becoming a market order, it becomes a limit order. This ensures your order will only be filled at your desired price or better, but there's a risk your order won't be filled if the price moves away quickly.
