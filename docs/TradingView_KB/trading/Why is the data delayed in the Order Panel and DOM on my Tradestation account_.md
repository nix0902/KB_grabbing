We receive Ask and Bid prices from the brokers once users log on their broker accounts on TradingView. In case the data for the symbol is delayed on broker platform, Ask and Bid quotes will be delayed as well. Data packages purchased on TradingView do not cover these quotes. They allow users to see real-time on charts, watchlist and screener ( for stocks). To see the Buy and Sell prices in real-time, users need to have subscription on the broker platform. We also support real-time data verification for Tradestation accounts, so users do not have to pay for data twice.
