To log on Tradovate account on TradingView, users need to enable the Add-on tools on the Tradovate platform. To do so, please open Tradovate platform: [https://trader.tradovate.com/](https://trader.tradovate.com/---%3E)→ click on Settings in the top right corner→select Add-on on top. Users with APEX and Elite Trader Funding accounts need to contact them and check if the Add-on subscription for TradingView has been correctly enabled for their accounts by the proprietary trading firms.
