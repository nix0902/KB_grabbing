Next, go to [The Leap](https://www.tradingview.com/the-leap) page, select a competition, and click "Join the contest." You'll see a confirmation pop-up with the main conditions. Just click the "Join" button to compete for cash prizes. **! Note:** Each competition has its own Terms and Conditions. Before registering, please read them. To master your skills, keep practicing risk-free with Paper Trading beyond The Leap. With it, you can trade almost any asset available on TradingView and gain deeper insights into what moves the markets.
