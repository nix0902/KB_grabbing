We employ a unique approach within the industry when determining a broker's rating: only verified users with active trading accounts linked to their respective brokers on TradingView are permitted to leave reviews. This rule guarantees that all feedback originates from genuine clients of the broker. Additionally, we assign greater importance to recent ratings and less importance to older ones. This enables users to make informed decisions in a competitive landscape by selecting brokers based on the most up-to-date feedback available. All these measures contribute to our mission of fostering an open and transparent trading community. No rating manipulation or other shady stuff allowed. Rate your preferred brokers with us, provide reviews, and assist them in maintaining their top-ranking positions today. Your votes make all the difference.
