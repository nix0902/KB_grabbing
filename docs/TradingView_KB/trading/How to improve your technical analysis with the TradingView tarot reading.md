Prepping before a tarot reading Set your intention Setting an intention is an important step in doing a tarot reading, as it helps to focus your mind and energy on the specific question or issue that you want to explore. Here are some tips on how to set an intention for a tarot reading. - **Be specific** When setting your intention, it's important to be as specific as possible. Instead of asking a general question like "Are there any hidden dangers to my portfolio right now?", try to focus on a specific area of your life, such as "Will my girlfriend find out about my meme-coin losses?" - **Be open** Be open to receive the guidance that the cards have to offer, even if it's not the answer you were hoping for. Sometimes the answers that we don't want to hear are the ones we need the most. - **Be positive** Try to frame your intention in a positive light. Instead of asking "What's going wrong with my trades?", try asking "How can I improve my relationship with financial markets?" - **Take some time to reflect beforehand** Before you start your reading, take some time to sit quietly and reflect on your question or intention. This will help you to clear your mind and be more receptive to the guidance that the cards have to offer. - **Write it down** Sometimes it can be helpful to write down your intention, it will help you to focus and clarify your thoughts. - **Trust the process** Remember that tarot readings are a tool for self-reflection and personal growth, and that the cards can offer guidance and insight that you may not have been able to see on your own. Trust the process, trust the cards, and trust yourself. By setting an intention for a tarot reading, you will be able to receive the guidance that you need, and to make the most of the reading, so that you can take action on the insights you gain. - **Cleanse your deck** All our decks undergo a three-step cleaning procedure — you can be sure that there is no negative energy left on the cards from previous uses of the deck. Additional tips - **Avoid drinking or drugs before a reading** It's generally not recommended to drink or do drugs before a tarot reading because they can impair your judgment and cloud your ability to interpret the cards accurately. When you are under the influence of alcohol or drugs, your mind is less clear and focused, which can make it more difficult to understand the symbolism and meanings of the cards. - **Trust your intuition** The tarot is a tool for gaining insight and guidance, but ultimately, the interpretation of the cards is up to you. Trust your intuition and allow yourself to be guided by the symbolism and imagery of the cards. - **Keep a journal** Keeping a record of your readings can help you to better understand the cards and your own interpretations of them. It's also a great way to track your progress and keep track of any patterns that may emerge over time. - **Be open-minded** The tarot can offer guidance and insight, but it's not always going to be what you expect or want to hear. Be open-minded and willing to consider different perspectives, even if they're uncomfortable or difficult to hear. - **Learn the card's meanings in different contexts** The tarot card meanings can vary depending on the context of the reading and the question being asked. It's important to understand the different ways a card can be interpreted and how it can change depending on the situation. Reading tarot: step-by-step guide - **Familiarize yourself with the meanings of the tarot cards** You can do this on the [TradingView tarot reading page](https://www.tradingview.com/tarot-reading/). - **Open your mind and set your intention for the reading** What do you hope to gain from it? Do you have a specific question in mind or are you looking for guidance on a certain issue? - **Focus on your question or intention and ask your question** You can ask the tarot for insight or guidance on a specific issue, or you can simply ask for a general reading. - **Pick three cards from the deck** Keep your attention on your question while you pick the cards. - **Interpret the cards in the spread** Consider the meaning of each card, as well as its position in the spread. Pay attention to any patterns or symbols that stand out to you. - **Reflect on the reading** Take some time to think about what the cards are trying to tell you. How do they relate to your question or intention? What insights or guidance do they offer? Disclaimer Remember, this isn't financial advice, just mystical advice.
