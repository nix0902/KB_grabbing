It is easy to stay organized if you trade in different currencies: create several accounts with the required currencies and switch between them directly in the trading panel. Accounts with different currencies simplify financial management and eliminate the need for complex calculations. This helps you better control your funds and plan your investments. All transactions, including profit and commission calculations, are carried out in the selected account currency, making trading more convenient. You can change the currency of your demo account in two ways. **Create a new account** and select a currency from the available list. Or **reset an existing account**. The system will then prompt you to choose a new currency. **! Note:** Resetting the account will delete the transaction history, active orders, and open positions.
