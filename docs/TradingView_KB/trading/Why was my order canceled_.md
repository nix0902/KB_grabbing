One of the reasons for order cancellation is its expiration. When order is placed Time in Force can be selected. There are four options: Day, Week, Month and GTD (Good till date) in Paper Trading. The Day option is applied by default and means that the order will get canceled in 24 hours unless it is not executed. Previous Previous Why can’t I place a Stop Order?
