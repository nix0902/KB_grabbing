- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / [ I can’t trade delayed symbols on TradeStation ](/support/solutions/43000758253-i-can-t-trade-delayed-symbols-on-tradestation/) # I can’t trade delayed symbols on TradeStation If you're unable to place orders on symbols with delayed data, it may be due to missing delayed data subscriptions on your broker account.Some exchanges (such as CBOT, COMEX, and NYMEX) charge a small fee for access to delayed quotes: Without an appropriate delayed data package, you will not receive price data from the broker - making trading unavailable.Please check your TradeStation market data subscriptions to ensure delayed data is enabled where required.
