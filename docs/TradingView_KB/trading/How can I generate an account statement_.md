Visit the [Tickmill Trader web application](https://tmt.tickmill.trade). Click the Menu icon in the top right corner, and select "Account Statement". Here you can choose a custom time range and instantly generate a detailed report for your trading account. All actions made on your TradingView account are synced with your Tickmill Trader account.
