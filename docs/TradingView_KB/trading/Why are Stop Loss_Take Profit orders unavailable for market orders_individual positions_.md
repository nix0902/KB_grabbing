- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / [ Why are Stop Loss/Take Profit orders unavailable for market order… ](/support/solutions/43000615813-why-are-stop-loss-take-profit-orders-unavailable-for-market-orders-individual-positions/) # Why are Stop Loss/Take Profit orders unavailable for market orders/individual positions? If you have a US account, the following broker restrictions apply: - Placing a market order with Stop Loss/Take Profit orders is not available. - It is not possible to add Stop Loss/Take Profit orders to each individual position. Adding Stop Loss/Take Profit orders is only possible to a Net position. - As a consequence: after the execution of a limit/stop order that has Stop Loss/Take Profit orders, these Stop Loss/Take Profit orders turn into a pair of linked OCO orders, but they are no longer linked to the opened individual position.
