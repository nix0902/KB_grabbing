- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Trading - / [ Trading basics ](/support/folders/43000597927-trading-basics/) - / [ Market orders ](/support/solutions/43000754940-market-orders/) # Market orders A market order is the most straightforward order type. It tells the broker to execute your trade at the best price immediately. It's the fastest way to enter or exit a position and is commonly used when you need certainty that your order will be filled. However, you have less control over the execution price, especially in fast-moving or low-liquidity markets.
