- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / [ Why can't I log into my paper trading account? ](/support/solutions/43000669304-why-can-t-i-log-into-my-paper-trading-account/) # Why can't I log into my paper trading account? You may not be able to log into your paper trading account if: - Your live account has been migrated to a different legal entity. In that case, your old paper trading account was closed; - You requested a new paper trading account that isn't ready. It normally takes 24 hours to be activated; - Your paper trading account has been closed due to inactivity; - Your live account is closed. In that case, your paper trading account will also be closed; - You have an older account and have never activated a paper account (newer accounts include a paper account).
