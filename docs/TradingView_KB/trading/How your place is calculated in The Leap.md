- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / Trading - / [ The Leap: general questions ](/support/folders/43000593579-the-leap-general-questions/) - / [ How your place is calculated in The Leap ](/support/solutions/43000721312-how-your-place-is-calculated-in-the-leap/) # How your place is calculated in The Leap Your place in the competition depends on the realized profit and loss (P&L) on your [Paper Trading](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000516466-paper-trading-main-functionality/) competition account. Realized P&L is the profit or loss from closed positions. The greater your realized profit, the higher your place. At the end of [The Leap](https://www.tradingview.com/the-leap/) competition, all open positions are automatically closed. The realized P&L from these positions is included in the final results of the competition.
