To connect your broker in the mobile app: - open the chart tab; - open More section by tapping on the three dots; - select Connect broker To log out of your broker in the mobile app: - open Account manager by tapping on the broker’s icon on the chart; - open the trading menu in the top right corner of the opened panel; - select the Log out option Previous Previous How to delete a created preset?
