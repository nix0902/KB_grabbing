- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / [ General questions (settings, data, and more) ](/support/folders/43000547584-general-questions-settings-data-and-more/) - / [ What does the Featured label mean? ](/support/solutions/43000701943-what-does-the-featured-label-mean/) # What does the Featured label mean? Featured is an exclusively reserved placement at the top of each broker list. You can see there in rotation our integrated partners with special Featured badges. Such a placement can be found on the homepage, on the "All Brokers" page, and on the trading panel. Only regulated brokers operating in multiple countries are entitled to the Featured badge. Therefore these partners are always well-known brands and trustworthy financial institutions. Try them yourself by checking if there are Featured brokers in your region.
