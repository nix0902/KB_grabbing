We are working hard on integrating new brokers on TradingView, however, the process takes time due to technical difficulties and regulations. Additionally, we’ve implemented the REST API, which allows new brokers to connect to TradingView. Brokers and crypto exchanges, which are not available on our platform yet, can use [this tool](https://www.tradingview.com/brokerage-integration/) for integration. You can contact the crypto exchange or the broker you wish to be connected to TradingView and suggest they connect to our interface using the above-mentioned API.
