[Log in to Paper Trading](https://www.tradingview.com/chart/?trade-now=Paper) and select the dedicated competition account from the Manage accounts menu. If you register for multiple competitions, you'll have separate accounts for each. **! Important:** Competition account parameters are fixed and cannot be changed. If you delete a competition account, all progress will be lost, and you won’t be able to re-register for the same competition. ### 1. Top Navigation Bar  
- **Tabs**: *Crypto Pairs Screener, Pine Editor, Strategy Tester, Replay Trading, Paper Trading (active), Trade* — used to switch between TradingView’s core tools.

  - *“The Leap (BY TRADESTATION) USD”* (current account, highlighted).  
  - *“Competition_user USD”* (another account).  
  - *“Create account…”* (option to make a new paper trading account).  
- **Platform Badge**: *“BY TRADESTATION”* (indicates the trading technology provider).  


### 3. Account Metrics (Left Panel)  
Displays key simulated account data:  
- *Account Balance*: 100,000.00 USD (total simulated funds).  
- *Equity*: 100,xxx.xx USD (total value of account, including open positions).  
- *Account Margin*: 0.00 USD (margin used for open positions).  
- *Available funds*: 100,000.00 USD (unallocated funds for new trades).  
- *Orders margin*: 0.00 USD (margin reserved for pending orders).  
- *Trading days*: 0 (days the account has been active).  


### 4. Tabs: Positions, Orders, History, Journal  
- **Positions (active)**: Shows open trades (current view).  
- **Orders**: Displays pending buy/sell orders.  
- **History**: Tracks past trades.  
- **Journal**: Logs trading activity/notes.  


### 5. Positions Table (Main Content)  
A table with columns for trade details (headers):  
- *Symbol*: Asset traded (e.g., stock/crypto ticker).  
- *Side*: Buy/Sell direction.  
- *Qty*: Quantity of the asset.  
- *Avg Fill Price*: Average price of the trade.  
- *Take Profit*: Target profit level.  
- *Stop Loss*: Risk management stop level.  
- *Last Price*: Most recent market price.  
- *Unrealized P&L*: Profit/loss on open positions (not yet realized).  
- *Trade Value*: Total value of the trade.  
- *Mark*: Additional marking/notes (truncated).  


### Purpose  
This interface is for **simulated trading** (paper trading) — users practice trading strategies with fake funds, track positions/orders, and monitor account performance without real money risk. The layout organizes account data, trade history, and real-time position details to support strategy testing and risk management.
