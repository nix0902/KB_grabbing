**Описание:** This is a **TradingView Paper Trading dropdown menu** (a mock trading environment for practice). Here’s a breakdown of all elements:  


### 1. Header & Account Info  
- **“Paper Trading”**: The main menu title (with a downward arrow, indicating it’s a dropdown).  
- **“Account: TradingView USD”**: Shows the active account (Paper Trading) and its currency (USD).  
- **Account Settings Icon**: A circular icon (likely for account configuration).  


### 2. Dropdown Menu Options (Left Column)  
A vertical list of actions for the Paper Trading account:  
- **“Trading settings…”**: Adjusts paper trading preferences (e.g., order types, slippage).  
- **“Restore confirmations…”**: Recovers past trade confirmation records.  
- **“Summary row”**: Toggles a summary row (e.g., for total P&L) in the trading interface.  
- **“Export data…”**: Exports trade history/data (e.g., to CSV).  
- **“See the brokers list…”**: Views brokers compatible with Paper Trading.  
- **“Log out Paper Trading”**: Logs out of the paper trading account (highlighted in red, indicating a critical action).  


### 3. Right Column (Table Headers)  
A partial table (likely for trade history) with columns:  
- **“Qty”**: Quantity of shares/contracts traded.  
- **“Avg Fill Price”**: Average price at which trades were executed.  


### Purpose  
This menu lets users manage their Paper Trading account (settings, data, logout) and view trade history (Qty, Avg Fill Price). It’s a core tool for practicing trading without real money.
