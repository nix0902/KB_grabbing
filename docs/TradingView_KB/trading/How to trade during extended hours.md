- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / Trading - / [ Paper Trading: general questions ](/support/folders/43000549214-paper-trading-general-questions/) - / [ How to trade during extended hours ](/support/solutions/43000647250-how-to-trade-during-extended-hours/) # How to trade during extended hours In both [Paper Trading](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000516466-paper-trading-main-functionality/) and trading with one of our [trusted brokers](https://www.tradingview.com/brokers/), you can place limit orders that may be executed outside the regular trading session. This allows you to test your strategies under conditions close to the real market, giving you additional flexibility when placing or adjusting orders. **! Note:** Trading on extended hours may be accompanied by increased volatility. Contact our support team if you have any questions or need assistance. The feature is available for the following instruments: - Stocks - ETFs - Bonds - Depositary receipts In the order panel, select the relevant option: - **Fill order outside RTH:** Allows the execution of limit orders during extended hours - **Fill take profit outside RTH:** Allows the execution of take profit orders during extended hours **! Note:** You can activate **Fill take profit outside RTH** only if a take profit order is enabled. To check which of your orders can be executed outside the regular trading hours, check the "Orders" tab of the trading panel.
