To start trading through BingX on TradingView you will need to do the following: - Log in on [www.tradingview.com](https://www.tradingview.com/) with your TradingView account; - Open a chart, click on the Trading Panel at the bottom and choose BingX; - Log in using your BingX account. Important note: Trading through BingX on TradingView is supported for USDT Standard Contract only. See below for more info on how to transfer funds to this type of account. **Описание:** This is a **Binance (TradingView-style) asset transfer interface** for moving crypto between accounts. Here’s a detailed breakdown:  


### **Top Navigation Bar**  
- **Left**: Binance logo + menu items: *Spot*, *Standard Contract*, *Perpetual Swap (Beta)*, *Buy Crypto*, *Market* (dropdown), *Community* (dropdown), *Reward Center*, *Blog*.  
- **Right**: *Assets* (dropdown, expanded to show *My Assets, Asset Record, Deposit, Withdraw, Transfer*), user profile icon, notification bell, download icon, *English* (language dropdown).  


### **Left Sidebar (Assets Menu)**  
- Header: *Assets*  
- Menu items (vertical list): *My Assets, Asset Record, Deposit, Withdraw, Transfer* (highlighted in blue, indicating the active page).  


### **Main Content: Transfer Form**  
- **Title**: *Transfer* (bold, centered).  
- **Form Fields**:  
  - *From*: Dropdown (default: *Fund Account*).  
  - *To*: Dropdown (default: *Standard Contract Ac*, highlighted with a blue border).  
    - Arrow icon between *From/To* indicates transfer direction.  
  - *Coin*: Dropdown (default: *USDT*).  
  - *Transfer Amount*: Text input (placeholder: *Transfer Amount*).  
  - *Max. Amount*: *1000* (displayed) + *All* (blue link, to auto-fill max).  
  - *Confirm*: Blue button (submits the transfer).  


### **Right Sidebar (Assets Dropdown)**  
- Mirrors the left sidebar’s *Assets* menu: *My Assets, Asset Record, Deposit, Withdraw, Transfer* (with *Transfer* highlighted, matching the active page).  


### **Bottom Right**  
- Two icons: User support (chain icon) and chat (speech bubble icon, blue background).  


### **Purpose**  
This interface lets users transfer USDT (or other cryptos) between Binance accounts (e.g., *Fund Account* → *Standard Contract Account*). The form guides users to select accounts, choose a coin, enter an amount, and confirm the transfer. The sidebar and top nav provide quick access to other asset management tasks (deposit, withdraw, view records).
