Limit orders are perfect when price matters more than speed. This type of order won't be executed unless the market reaches your specified price or better, which gives you more control over the transaction. However, there's no guarantee it will be filled if the market doesn't reach your limit price. Previous Previous Market orders Next Next Stop orders Launch Supercharts
