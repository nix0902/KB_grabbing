- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / [ I'd like to place orders without having to confirm them every tim… ](/support/solutions/43000480920-i-d-like-to-place-orders-without-having-to-confirm-them-every-time/) # I'd like to place orders without having to confirm them every time You can enable “One-click trading” (“One-tap trading” for mobile devices) in your Trading settings: When it’s active, all order-related actions — placing, modifying, canceling orders, or closing positions — are performed immediately, without confirmation windows or the Order Ticket. One-click / One-tap orders can be placed via: - The Buy/Sell buttons - The “+” menu on the price scale - The context menu (right-click on the chart) - Built-in keyboard shortcuts - Order presets, which automatically apply predefined order parameters like quantity, order type, and stop/limit levels Note: With “One-click trading” enabled, all actions are sent immediately after clicking.
