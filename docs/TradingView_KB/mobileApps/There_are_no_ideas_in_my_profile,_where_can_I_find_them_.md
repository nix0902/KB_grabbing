# There are no ideas in my profile, where can I find them? 
 If you don't see published ideas in your profile, there are two possible reasons:

- You might have published the idea on a different language version of the site (subdomain). Try changing the language to the one that you had when you published the idea (see details [here](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000506671)).
- If you are unable to see the idea that you've just published, try updating the profile section (slide down), restarting the app or waiting 5-15 mins.
