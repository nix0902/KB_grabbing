Here are the steps that you need to follow. - Download the latest version of the app on the [AppStore](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/tradingview-trading-community-charts-and-quotes/id1205990992). - Log into your TradingView account. - Allow the app to send notifications in the settings. In order to enable this, you need to go to Settings ** **-&gt;** ** Notifications ** **-&gt;** ** Select TradingView App ** **-&gt;** ** Turn on Allow Notifications . - Tick the Push Notifications box when creating an alert. Alerts that you create in your mobile app will have this feature enabled by default. And that's it! This feature is available to all users, regardless of plan type.
