# How do I remove or customize an indicator? 
 To remove an indicator or see its settings, open the chart legend by clicking the arrow button under the symbol name:

Complete the following steps afterwards:

- Click the trash icon to remove an indicator;
- Click the gear icon to change the settings of an indicator.
