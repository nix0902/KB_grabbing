# How do I enable push notifications to receive alerts on my Android device? 
 Here is what you need to do to start receiving push notifications through your Android app:

- Download [the latest version of the app on Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.tradingview.tradingviewapp);
- Log in with your credentials;
- Allow the TradingView app to send push notifications in the settings. In order to enable this, you need to go to *Settings -> Notifications -> Select TradingView App -> Turn on Allow Notifications*;
- Tick the *Push notifications* box when creating an alert.

Alerts that you create in your mobile app will have this feature enabled by default. *D**on’t forget to whitelist the app on your Android device*. Note that push notifications are available to all users, regardless of plan type.
