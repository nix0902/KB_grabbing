There are two ways to hide or make the indicator visible again: **Option 1.** Click on the eye icon in the indicator legend: **Option 2.** - Open the Analysis Hub menu (near Drawings icon) in the lower toolbar; - Scroll it down; - Click on the Object Tree icon: - Select the necessary indicator and change its visibility settings in the pop-up window: You can edit your drawing tools in the same way.
