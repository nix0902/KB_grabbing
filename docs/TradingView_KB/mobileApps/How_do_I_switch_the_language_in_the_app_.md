# How do I switch the language in the app? 
 To switch the language in the iOS or Andriod app, you need to complete the steps below:

- Open the *Menu* tab;
- Open the *Settings menu*;
- Open the *Language* menu;
- Select your preferred language;

The app will change the language automatically.

Please note that your selected language will apply to the list of public chats as well as the ideas feed.
