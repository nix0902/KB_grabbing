# Watchlist advanced view mode 
 Watchlists can show you much more than just price data and its changes. By accessing the advanced view mode, you can dive deeper into the analysis of your selected symbols.

#### How to access the advanced view mode 

From any page of TradingView, when you open your watchlist, click the pie chart icon in the upper right corner.

#### 

#### **Advanced view tabs**

With tabs, you can turn your watchlist into a screening tool. The data is organized into several sections, each providing an overview of different aspects of your symbols.

#### **Overview**

In the overview section, you can find various useful metrics for your watchlist. The table has several modes:

- Price: Key data with price values, price change, trading volume, and market capitalization
- Financials: Fundamental metrics such as revenue, EPS, P/E ratio, beta, dividend, and yield
- Performance: Price change in percent over different time periods
- Risk: Beta and volatility across different timeframes
- Technicals: Key technical indicators and ratings

#### Price 

In this tab, you can find key data points related to a symbol's price and its changes:

- Last: Current instrument price in instrument currency
- Chg%: (Close price of the current bar - Close price of the previous bar) / Close price of the previous bar × 100
- Chg: Close price of the current bar - Close price of the previous bar
- Volume: Trading volume for the day
- Avg Vol (10): Average trading volume over the past 10 days
- [Market Cap](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000597015-market-capitalization/): Total market value of a company

#### Financials 

View key stats from corporate reports:

- [Revenue](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000553619-total-revenue-sales/): Amount of money a company generates from selling its goods or services over a specific period
- [EPS](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000744068-reported-eps/): Profit a company makes for each outstanding share of its common stock
- [P/E Ratio](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000597017-price-to-earnings-ratio/): Market price of a stock relative to its earnings per share
- [Beta](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000703603/): Measure of a symbol’s sensitivity to changes in the overall market
- [Dividend](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000670334-dividends-per-share-common-stock-primary-issue/): Cash payment a company distributes to shareholders
- [Yield](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000597817-dividend-yield/): Ratio of a company’s annualized dividend to its share price

#### Performance 

This tab displays percentage price change over different time periods:

- One week
- One month
- Three months
- Year-to-date
- One year
- Five years
- All time

#### Risk 

- [Beta](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000703603-beta/) 1Y / 3Y / 5Y: Measures the asset's sensitivity to market movements
- Volatility 1D / 1W / 1M: Measures the price variations of a financial instrument over a specified period

#### Technicals 

Displays summary of key technical indicators:

- [Tech rating](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000614331-technical-ratings/): Combined rating based on several technical indicators
- [MA](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000502589-moving-averages/) Rating: Suggestion based on moving averages
- Os Rating: Suggestion based on oscillators
- [RSI](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000502338-relative-strength-index-rsi/) (14): Momentum indicator showing potential overbought or oversold conditions
- [Mom](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000589187-momentum/) (10): Momentum oscillator used to determine market trends
- [AO](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000501826-awesome-oscillator-ao/): Awesome Oscillator — measures market momentum
- [CCI](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000502001-commodity-channel-index-cci/) (20): Oscillator identifying overbought and oversold levels by measuring deviations from the statistical mean

#### 

#### **Earnings**

This section displays upcoming earnings reports. It includes the following fields:

- Date: Earnings report date
- Time: Estimated or actual report time
- Estimate EPS: Estimated earnings per share
- Actual EPS: Actual earnings per share (if already published)
- Surprise: Difference between actual and estimated earnings, in %
- Market cap: Company’s market value

#### **Dividends**

This section displays the [dividend calendar](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000759913-introduction-to-dividend-calendar/) for the next 14 days.

The table can be generated by:

- Ex-dividend date: The last date on which shareholders are eligible to receive the most recent dividend
- Payment date: The date when a company actually pays the declared dividend to shareholders

The table includes the following fields:

- Amount: How much a company is expected to pay
- Ex-dividend date: The cutoff date by which the stock must be held to receive the dividend.
- Payment date: When the company is expected to pay the dividend
- Dividend yield: Dividend expressed as a percentage of the share price

#### **News**

In this section, you can find all the latest news about the symbols in your watchlist.

#### 

#### 

#### **Additional options**

In the upper-right corner of the table, you can find control elements that offer extra interaction options.

#### **Currency convertion to USD**

If your watchlist includes symbols denominated in currencies other than USD, you can easily convert them.

#### **Export list**

Click this button to export a .txt file containing the names of watchlist sections and tickers.

#### **Summary**

Adds a “TOTAL” summary row that displays aggregated values across all symbols:

- Minimum
- Maximum
- Average
- Median

! Note: The summary operates on absolute values and doesn't account for currency conversion, percentage values, etc.

#### **Group by**

Here, you can organize your watchlist by the following criteria:

- No group
- Custom sections
- Currency
- Symbol type
- Sector
- Exchange

#### 

#### Oh, and one more advanced view thing… 

Below the table, a widget visualizes symbol distribution. Two pie charts show the composition of your watchlist by symbol type and sector.
