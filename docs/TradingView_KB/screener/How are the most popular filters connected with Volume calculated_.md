"Change" is the difference between the current and the previous bars. Hence, if the 1W time interval is applied, then change = Close of the current weekly bar - Close of the previous weekly bar (you can see these bars on the chart if you select the weekly chart timeframe). **Volume 24h Change** Volume 24h Change = Volume 24h now - Volume 24h day ago **Volume 24h Change %** Volume 24h Change % = Volume 24h Change/Volume 24h day ago * 100 The "Volume 24h Change %" displays the percentage change in volume over the past 24 hours and may indirectly indicate market moves. **Volume Change** Volume Change = Volume of the current bar - Volume of the previous bar **Volume Change %** Volume Change % = Volume Change/Volume of the previous bar * 100 **Price * Volume (Turnover)** Price * Volume (Turnover) = Close of the current bar * Volume of the current bar Previous Previous How are the most popular filters connected with Pre-market and Post-market calculated?
