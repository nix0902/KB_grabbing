- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / [ Sharia-compliant securities ](/support/solutions/43000723733-sharia-compliant-securities/) # Sharia-compliant securities Sharia-compliant (halal) securities are investment instruments (such as stocks or bonds) of companies or issuers whose activities comply with the rules and principles of the Islamic financial system. Key aspects of Islamic economic principles include the prohibition of interest income (riba), the prohibition of investments related to the production of alcohol, tobacco, weapons, gambling, and other activities and products prohibited in Islam, as well as the requirement of fairness and ethics in investments. Trading in such securities is available both on exchanges in Islamic countries and on major international platforms.
