# How are the most popular filters connected with Pre-market and Post-market calculated? 
 In screener you can see pricing data during all trading day divided into pre-market, regular session and post-market. You can analyse the data such as Pre-market and Post-market Change/Change %, Pre- and Post-market Open, High, Low, Price and Volume. 

#### There are several features about which it is necessary to know before analyzing data in pre- and post-market: 

1. The data in the pre- and post-market columns is always only for the last trading session of the market. In other words, the data in them is deleted at the start of a new premarket session.

2.The data of the post-market is available only when the post-market lasts. So, if the regular session hasn’t begun, the columns with postmarket fields will be empty.

Below the formulas of main Pre-market and Post-market fields are described:

**Pre-market Change and Pre-market Change %**

 Pre-market Change = Close price of the pre-market - Close price of the previous regular session 

 Pre-market Change % = (Pre-market Change / Close price of the previous regular session) * 100 **Post-market Change and Post-market Change %**

 Post-market Change = Close price of the post-market - Close price of the previous regular session 

 Post-market Change % = (Post-market Change / Close price of the previous regular session) * 100 **Pre-market Change from Open and Pre-market Change from Open %**

 Pre-market Change from Open = Close price of the pre-market - Open price of the pre-market 

 Pre-market Change from Open% = Pre-market Change from Open / Open price of the pre-market * 100 **Pre-market Gap %**

 Pre-market Gap % = (Open price of the pre-market - Close price of the previous regular session) / Close price of the previous regular session * 100 

**Pre-market Open**

 Pre-market Open = The symbol opening price in the pre-market session **Pre-market High**

 Pre-market High = The highest symbol price in the pre-market session **Pre-market Low**

 Pre-market Low = The lowest symbol price in the pre-market session **Pre-market Price**

 Pre-market Price = The symbol closing price in the pre-market session **Pre-market Volume**

 Pre-market Volume = The number of trading units (shares, contracts, etc) for the symbol in the pre-market session **Post-market Open**

 Post-market Open = The symbol opening price in the post-market session **Post-market High**

 Post-market High = The highest symbol price in the post-market session **Post-market Low**

 Post-market Low = The lowest symbol price in the post-market session **Post-market Price**

 Post-market Price = The symbol closing price in the post-market session **Post-market Volume**
