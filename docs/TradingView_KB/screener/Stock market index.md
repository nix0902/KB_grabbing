Each index is typically designed to represent a particular market segment — for example, the largest companies, a specific industry, or a geographic region. Investors use indices to: - Track overall market trends (e.g., "Is the market going up or down?"); - Benchmark performance of portfolios or individual stocks; - Gain diversified exposure to a group of stocks through index funds or ETFs. To scan stocks by the stock market index, add the “Index” filter. The screener will display only the stocks that are currently included in the selected indices: The filter depends on the selected market. If you change the market, the options in the Index filter will be adjusted according to the market criteria. If no filter options are available, it means that this market does not have corresponding indices. **Описание:** This is a **TradingView Stock Screener** interface focused on the **S&P 500 index**, displaying a list of stocks with their key metrics. Here’s a detailed breakdown:  


### **Top Navigation & Filters**  
- **Header**:  
  - *Stock Screener* (dropdown menu for screeners) | *Save* (cloud icon, saves current screen) | *Theme toggle* (hexagon) | *Expand/collapse* (chevron).  
- **Filter Buttons** (horizontal row, left to right):  
  - *Market* (US flag, dropdown) | *Watchlist* | *Index* | *Price* | *Change %* | *Market cap* | *P/E* | *EPS dil growth* | *Div yield %* | *Sector* | *Analyst Rating* | *Perf %* | *Revenue growth* | *PEG* | *ROE* | *Beta* | *Recent earnings date* | *Upcoming earnings date*.  


### **Index Selection Dropdown**  
- A black pill-shaped button labeled *Index 500 S&P 500* (with a dropdown arrow) is active.  
- A dropdown menu is open, showing:  
  - *Reset* (X icon, clears selection) | *Search* bar | List of indices (e.g., SPX · S&P 500, NDX · NASDAQ 100, DJI · Dow Jones Industrial Average, etc.) with checkboxes.  
  - *Select all* (checked) | *Remove* (trash icon) at the bottom.  


### **Tab Bar**  
- Tabs: *Overview* (active, bold) | *Performance* | *Dividends* | *Profitability* | *More* (dropdown).  
- *Refresh* icon (right of tabs) to reload data.  


### **Stock List Table**  
- **Columns** (left to right):  
  - *Symbol* (e.g., AAPL, MSFT) with company logos.  
  - *Price* (e.g., 208.91 USD).  
  - *Market cap* (e.g., 3.14T USD).  
  - *P/E* (e.g., 33.21).  
  - *EPS dil TTM* (e.g., 6.29 USD).  
  - *EPS dil growth TTM YoY* (e.g., -2.13%, red for negative).  
  - *Div yield %* (e.g., 0.48%).  
- **Rows**: Listed stocks (AAPL, MSFT, NVDA, AMZN, etc.) with their respective metrics.  


### **Key UI Elements**  
- *Grid/List toggle* (left of *Overview* tab) | *Add column* (+ icon, right of table) | *Search bar* (top-left, “Symbol 503”).  


### **Purpose**  
This screen lets users **filter and analyze S&P 500 stocks** using customizable metrics (price, P/E, earnings growth, etc.). The dropdown allows switching indices, and tabs organize data by category (overview, performance, dividends). The table displays real-time stock data for comparison.
