- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / [ Stock exchange’s markets ](/support/solutions/43000753340-stock-exchange-s-markets/) # Stock exchange’s markets Stock exchanges often divide traded assets into separate markets (market segments) to classify companies based on specific criteria. This helps investors navigate trading more effectively and select securities based on their goals and strategies. Main criteria for market segmentation: - company capitalization; - liquidity and turnover requirements; - company type and industry segment; - regional classification. To scan stocks by the exchange’s market, add the "Exchange's market" filter. In the added filter, choose one or more exchange’s markets: To disable this filter, click the Reset button: The filter depends on the selected market. If you change the market, the options in the Exchange's market filter will be adjusted according to the market criteria. If no filter options are available, it means that this market does not have a corresponding exchange. **Описание:** This TradingView image displays the **Stock Screener** interface with a focus on filtering by exchange market (OTCQB). Here’s a detailed breakdown:  


### **Top Navigation & Controls**  
- **Header**: “Stock Screener” (dropdown menu) + “Save” button (cloud icon) + settings (gear) + expand/collapse (chevron) icons.  
- **Filter Bar**: Horizontal row of dropdown filters (Market: US, Watchlist, Index, Price, Change %, Market cap, P/E, EPS dil growth, Div yield %, Sector, Analyst Rating, Perf %, Revenue growth, PEG, ROE, Beta, Recent earnings date, Upcoming earnings date).  
- **Exchange Filter**: “Exchange’s Market OTCQB” (dropdown) + “+” (add filter) + “Reset all” (clear all filters).  


### **Chart/Tab Section**  
- **Tab Bar**: “Overview” (active), “Performance”, “Extended H” (truncated).  
- **View Toggle**: Grid (list) / line (chart) icons.  
- **Reset Dropdown**: Opened from “Exchange’s Market OTCQB”, showing:  
  - “EXCHANGE’S MARKET” header.  
  - Checkboxes: *OTCQB (checked)*, OTCQX, Pink.  
  - “Remove” button (trash icon) to delete the filter.  


### **Results Table**  
- **Search Bar**: “Symbol” (with “948” entered) + “Price” column header.  
- **Columns**: Symbol, Name (with “D” for dividend), Price, Change % (color-coded: red = down, green = up), Volume, Market cap, P/E.  
- **Rows**: List of stocks (e.g., PLDGP, LBRDB, FNMA, FNMAO, etc.) with:  
  - Ticker, company name, price, % change, volume, market cap, P/E ratio.  
  - Example: *FNMAO (Fannie Mae)* shows -5.74% (red), 2.2K volume, 7.09B market cap, 33,500 P/E.  


### **Purpose**  
The interface lets users filter stocks by exchange (OTCQB) and other criteria, view real-time price/volume data, and analyze performance via color-coded changes. The “Reset” dropdown manages the active exchange filter, while the table displays filtered results with key metrics.
