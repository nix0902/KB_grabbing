# How to scan global stocks 
 With the Entire world screener feature, you can access the global stock view mode, where you can scan 70+ markets across 50+ countries.

This feature helps you:

- Scan the entire world at once
- Scan specific markets
- Scan a single market

In the [Stock Screener](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000718866/), select the globe or flag icon. 

Then select “Entire world” to view all currently available stock markets.

You can also toggle the “Multi-select” option to choose several specific countries.
 Or view your favorite stocks from just one market. To clear your selections, hit the “Reset” button to return to your default market.

 **! Note:** When switching markets, make sure to review the [currency conversion](/support/solutions/43000639101/) principles. Previous Previous Currency conversion in the Stock Screener Next Next How can I change the chart view grid? Launch Supercharts
