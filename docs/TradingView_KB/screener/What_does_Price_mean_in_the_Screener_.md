# What does Price mean in the Screener? 
 If you want to find the condition of an indicator to the current symbol price (e.g. stocks that are traded above their SMA10), which is indicated as "Close" on the chart, use the "Price" value in the Screener. Please note that the "Price" value in the screener is the symbol's regular session value. If you enable extended hours on the chart, the "Price" of the screener and the "Close" of the chart will not match.
