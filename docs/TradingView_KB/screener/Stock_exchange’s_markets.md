# Stock exchange’s markets 
 Stock exchanges often divide traded assets into separate markets (market segments) to classify companies based on specific criteria. This helps investors navigate trading more effectively and select securities based on their goals and strategies.
Main criteria for market segmentation:

- company capitalization;
- liquidity and turnover requirements;
- company type and industry segment;
- regional classification.

To scan stocks by the exchange’s market, add the "Exchange's market" filter. In the added filter, choose one or more exchange’s markets:

To disable this filter, click the Reset button:

The filter depends on the selected market. If you change the market, the options in the Exchange's market filter will be adjusted according to the market criteria. If no filter options are available, it means that this market does not have a corresponding exchange.
