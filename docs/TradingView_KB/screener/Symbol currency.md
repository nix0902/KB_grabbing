Global companies often list on multiple exchanges in different local currencies. This filter helps manage currency exposure or focus on assets priced in a specific currency relevant to your portfolio or region. To screen assets by their trading currency: -Add the “Symbol Currency” filter to your screener setup. In the added filter, select one or more currencies from the list. The screener will then only show assets priced in the selected currency/currencies. The filter depends on the selected market. If you change the market, the options in the Symbol Currency filter will be adjusted according to the market criteria. To disable this filter and see assets regardless of their trading currency, click the Reset button. Previous Previous Exchange Next Next Issue and Coupon currency Launch Supercharts
