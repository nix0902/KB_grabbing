You can see three predicted price levels: minimum, maximum and average. **Where does the forecast come from?** FactSet provides analyst recommendations and their consensus score is a predictive measure of future price movement, giving insight into market expectations for each security. For example, the screenshot shows 38 analysts' yearly consensus price prediction for NASDAQ:AAPL on November 28, 2022.
