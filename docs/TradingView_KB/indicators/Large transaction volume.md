Large transaction volume represents the total volume of transactions with a value of $100,000 or greater for a particular cryptocurrency. Why is "Large transaction volume" important? Since this sum of money is not available to the average retail trader, the "Large transaction volume" indicator allows investors to track the transactions of major market players and focus on them when making investment decisions. High Large transaction volume typically indicates institutional activity, whale movements, or significant capital flows that can precede major price movements. Monitoring this metric helps traders identify when smart money is accumulating or distributing, providing valuable insights into potential market direction changes. How is "Large transaction volume" calculated? Large transaction volume is calculated by summing the total value of all transactions with individual transaction values ≥ $100,000 during the measurement period.
