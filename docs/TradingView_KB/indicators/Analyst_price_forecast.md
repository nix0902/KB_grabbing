# Analyst price forecast 
 The price forecast displayed on the chart of the [Forecast tab](https://www.tradingview.com/symbols/NASDAQ-AAPL/forecast/) of the symbol page reflects the dynamics of price changes over the past 2 years and the forecast of possible price behavior by the end of the next year. You can see three predicted price levels: minimum, maximum and average.

**Where does the forecast come from?**
FactSet provides analyst recommendations and their consensus score is a predictive measure of future price movement, giving insight into market expectations for each security.

For example, the screenshot shows 38 analysts' yearly consensus price prediction for NASDAQ:AAPL on November 28, 2022.

Forecast data is available for a number of symbols from the following exchanges: ADX, AMEX, ASX, ATHEX, BAHRAIN, BCBA, BCS, BER, BET, BIST, BME, BMFBOVESPA, BMV, BSE, BVB, BVC, BVL, BX, CSE, DFM, DUS, EGX, EURONEXT, FSE, FWB, GPW, HAM, HAN, HKEX, HNX, HOSE, IDX, JSE, KRX, LSE, LSIN, LUXSE, MIL, MUN, MYX, NAG, NASDAQ, NEO, NGM, NSE, NSENG, NYSE, NZX, OMXCOP, OMXHEX, OMXSTO, OMXTSE, OSL, OTC, PSE, QSE, SAPSE, SET, SGX, SIX, SSE, SWB, SZSE, TADAWUL, TASE, TPEX, TSE, TSX, TSXV, TWSE, UPCOM, XETR.

Remember that the forecast is not an investment recommendation or a guide to action. In investments and trading, you should not make any decisions without your own evaluation of the instrument.

**Описание:**

This TradingView interface displays a **price target forecast** for Apple Inc. (AAPL), combining historical price data with analyst projections. Here’s a detailed breakdown of all elements:  


### 1. Header Section  
- **Title**: *“Price target”* (bold, black text) – indicates the chart’s focus.  
- **Price Target Value**: *“175.69 USD”* (large, bold black text) – the average 1-year price forecast from analysts.  
- **Change Metrics**:  
  - *“+29.98”* (green text) – absolute increase in the price target.  
  - *“+20.58%”* (green text) – percentage increase in the price target.  
- **Analyst Context**: *“The 38 analysts offering 1 year price forecasts for Apple Inc have a max estimate of 200.00 and a min estimate of 122.00.”* (black text) – explains the data source (38 analysts) and range of forecasts.  


### 2. Chart Area  
The chart has two segments: **“PAST 2Y”** (historical data) and **“1Y FORECAST”** (future projections).  

#### A. X-Axis (Time)  
- Labels: *“2021”*, *“May”*, *“Sep”*, *“2022”*, *“May”*, *“Sep”*, *“2023”*, *“May”*, *“Aug”*, *“13”* – marks time periods (past 2 years + 1-year forecast).  
- Segments:  
  - *“PAST 2Y”* (white background, left) – shows historical price movement.  
  - *“1Y FORECAST”* (white background, right) – shows projected price ranges.  


#### B. Y-Axis (Price)  
- Values: *“100.00”*, *“140.00”*, *“160.00”*, *“180.00”*, *“200.00”* – price levels in USD.  


#### C. Data Series & Annotations  
- **Blue Line (Historical Price)**: Tracks Apple’s stock price from 2021 to early 2023 (left segment, “PAST 2Y”).  
- **Forecast Ranges (Right Segment, “1Y FORECAST”)**:  
  - **Max Estimate**:  
    - Label: *“Max +37.23%”* (green text) – percentage increase from the current price.  
    - Value: *“200.00”* (green box) – highest analyst forecast.  
    - Visual: Light green shaded area (top range) with a dashed line.  
  - **Average Estimate**:  
    - Label: *“Avg +20.55%”* (green text) – percentage increase (matches the header’s “+20.58%” with minor rounding).  
    - Value: *“175.69”* (green box) – average of 38 analyst forecasts.  
    - Visual: Light green shaded area (middle range) with a dashed line.  
  - **Current Price**:  
    - Label: *“Current”* (blue text) – current stock price.  
    - Value: *“145.74”* (blue box) – the price at the start of the forecast period.  
    - Visual: Blue horizontal line (baseline for forecasts).  
  - **Min Estimate**:  
    - Label: *“Min –16.29%”* (red text) – percentage decrease from the current price.  
    - Value: *“122.00”* (red box) – lowest analyst forecast.  
    - Visual: Light red shaded area (bottom range) with a dashed line.  


### 3. UI Elements & Their Purposes  
- **Text Labels**: Explain data (e.g., “Max +37.23%” clarifies the max forecast’s percentage change).  
- **Color-Coded Boxes/Text**:  
  - Green: Positive forecasts (max, average).  
  - Blue: Current price.  
  - Red: Negative forecast (min).  
- **Shaded Areas**: Visually distinguish forecast ranges (green = upside, red = downside).  
- **Dashed Lines**: Connect forecast labels to their corresponding price levels on the chart.  


### Overall Purpose  
This interface summarizes **analyst consensus** for Apple’s 1-year stock price, showing historical performance, current price, and the range of projected prices (max, average, min) with percentage changes. It helps traders/investors assess potential upside/downside and the average expected return.
