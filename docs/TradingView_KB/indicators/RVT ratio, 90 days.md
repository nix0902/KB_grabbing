RVT ratio (Realized Value to Transaction Volume Ratio, 90-day adjusted) compares realized capitalization to transaction volume using a 90-day moving average. The realized cap values each coin at the price it last moved rather than the current market price, offering a more stable baseline for valuation. Why is "RVT ratio, 90 days" important? This metric provides a high-conviction indicator for long-term market cycles. High RVT values suggest overvaluation and potential market tops, while low values indicate undervaluation and accumulation zones. The 90-day adjustment filters out short-term noise, making it particularly useful for identifying macro-trend changes. Traders use this metric to time major market cycles and distinguish between sustainable trends and temporary price spikes. How is "RVT ratio, 90 days" calculated?
