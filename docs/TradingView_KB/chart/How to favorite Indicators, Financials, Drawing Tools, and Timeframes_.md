For quick access to frequently-used tools, you can add them to Favorites. To do this, just click on the star symbol located next to the name or icon of this tool. Now your favorite tools are just one click away. Drawing tools are in the toolbar right on your chart. Analysis tools are in the dropdown menu or in the Favorites tab.
