- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / [ How to draw temporarily on the chart - Demonstration cursor ](/support/solutions/43000747626-how-to-draw-temporarily-on-the-chart-demonstration-cursor/) # How to draw temporarily on the chart - Demonstration cursor The Demonstration cursor is similar to the Highlighter tool. You can draw and mark something on the chart - the drawings will fade over some time. This tool may be useful during live demonstrations, on a stream, or when recording video ideas. It draws your audience’s attention to key chart areas without manually erasing temporary highlights. The Demonstration cursor is in the Cursors menu of the drawings toolbar on the left side of the chart. Hold Alt /⌥ key and click on the desired area to draw freely within the active chart. You can place dots by clicking in places you want to point at, or create more complex notes by holding the left mouse button. The drawn elements will gradually disappear after a few seconds.
