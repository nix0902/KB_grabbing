- [ Help Center ](/) - / [ Knowledge base ](/knowledge-base/) - / Chart - / [ Hotkeys and tips ](/support/folders/43000561752-hotkeys-and-tips/) - / [ What are indicator templates ](/support/solutions/43000543048-what-are-indicator-templates/) # What are indicator templates Indicator templates are a way of grouping several indicators together into a single package, which you can later apply to any chart. You can also create your own templates and save them to your own database. TradingView comes with six built-in indicator templates: - Bill Williams' 3 Lines - Displaced EMA - MA Exp Ribbon - Oscillators - Swing Trading - Volume Based This is an example of a CORN chart that utilizes the Oscillator Indicator template, which includes: - [Volume](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000591617/) - [Relative Strength Index (RSI)](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000502338-relative-strength-index-rsi/) - [Stochastic RSI (STOCH RSI)](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000502333-stochastic-rsi-stoch-rsi/) - [Commodity Channel Index (CCI)](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000502001-commodity-channel-index-cci/) Here is the same CORN chart but with the Bill Williams' 3 Lines Indicator template, which includes: - [Volume](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000591617/) - Three [Moving Averages](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000502589-moving-averages/) of different length How to use indicator templates To use a preset indicator template, simply click on the corresponding button on the toolbar along the top of the chart and click on the template that you want to use. You can also save your custom indicator templates. To do that, choose the desired indicators so they are displayed on the chart. Then click on the "Indicator Template" button and select "Save indicator template." **Описание:** The image shows a **TradingView chart interface** for Apple Inc. (AAPL) on the NASDAQ exchange, with a focus on the **Indicators dropdown menu** open. Here’s a detailed breakdown:  


### 1. **Top Bar & Ticker Info**  
- **Ticker & Exchange**: “Apple Inc · 1D · NASDAQ” (1D = 1-day timeframe) with a green dot (live data) and a pink “~” (no major news).  
- **Price**: “222.52 +0.39 (+0.18%)” (current price, change, and percentage change).  


### 2. **Indicators Dropdown Menu**  
The menu (open via the “Indicators” button) lists pre-built indicator templates:  
- **“Save Indicator template…”**: Option to save custom indicator setups.  
- **“RECENTLY USED” Section**:  
  - *MA Exp Ribbon*: Exponential Moving Averages (x8) + Volume.  
  - *Displaced EMA*: Exponential Moving Average + Volume.  
  - *Bill Williams’ 3 Lines*: Moving Averages (x3) + Volume.  
  - *Debt and solvency*: Financial ratios (debt-to-assets, debt-to-equity, etc.).  
  - *Dividends*: Dividend-related metrics (payout ratio, yield, etc.).  
- **“Open template…”**: Load saved indicator templates.  


### 3. **Chart Area**  
- **Price Chart**: A line chart (purple) showing AAPL’s price movement over time (x-axis: Nov → Dec → 2025 → Feb).  
- **Grid Lines**: Horizontal/vertical lines for price/time reference.  


### 4. **Left Toolbar (Drawing/Tools)**  
Icons for:  
- Zoom (magnifying glass), trend lines, Fibonacci, chart types, text, ruler, search, magnet (snap), scissors (cut), lock (security), eye (visibility), link (share).  


### 5. **Bottom Timeframe Bar**  
- **Timeframes**: 1D, 5D, 1M, 3M, 6M, YTD, 1Y, 5Y, All (select chart duration).  
- **Calendar Icon**: Open date/time selector.  


### 6. **Top Right Controls**  
- **Alert**: Set price alerts.  
- **Replay**: Replay historical price action.  
- **Navigation Arrows**: Undo/redo actions.  
- **TradingView Logo**: Toggle platform menu.  


### Purpose  
This interface lets traders analyze AAPL’s price action, add technical indicators (via the dropdown), adjust timeframes, and use drawing tools—all core to technical analysis on TradingView.
