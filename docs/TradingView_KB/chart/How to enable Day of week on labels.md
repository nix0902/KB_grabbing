- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / Chart - / [ I can't find a certain feature or setting ](/support/folders/43000547370-i-can-t-find-a-certain-feature-or-setting/) - / [ How to enable Day of week on labels ](/support/solutions/43000692911-how-to-enable-day-of-week-on-labels/) # How to enable Day of week on labels - Open the Chart Settings by clicking on the gear button on the top panel of the chart, or by double-clicking on the bars (candlesticks). - Go to the Scales and lines tab. - Check the box next to “Day of week on labels” The day of the week will be displayed on the timeline label.
