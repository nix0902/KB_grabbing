It prevents screeners, calendars, options, and other widgets from overlapping your chart, allowing for less noise and greater flexibility. ! Note: Split view mode is only available on Supercharts. To test the convenience of this mode, find the widgets icons on the right toolbar and open the one you need for your analysis. - [Screeners](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000718885-quick-and-easy-guide-to-tradingview-screeners/) - [Calendars](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000707391-economic-calendar-features-overview/) - [Options](https://www.tradingview.com/support/solutions/43000707214-options-strategy-builder-overview/) After the side panel is displayed, press the Move overlay to split-view button. By default, the panel will take 50% of the screen size, but you can toggle it according to your needs. You can repeat this with other widgets and pin all three in your workspace. As you see, the widget icons disappear as you split view them. The size and the order of the widget windows are saved and will reemerge the next time you open the chart.
