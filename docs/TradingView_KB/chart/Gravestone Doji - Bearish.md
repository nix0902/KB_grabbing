It is formed when a doji forms at the day’s low point or close to it, with a long upper shadow that signals a bearish trend. When it appears at market top it is considered a reversal signal. The bullish version of the Gravestone Doji pattern is the [Dragonfly Doji pattern](https://www.tradingview.com/chart/?solution=43000583768).
