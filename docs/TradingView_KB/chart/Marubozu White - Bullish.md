Marubozu is Japanese for “close-cropped” or “close-cut.” Other sources may call it a Bald or Shaven Head Candle. The bearish version of this pattern is the [Marubozu Black candlestick pattern](https://www.tradingview.com/chart/?solution=43000583789).
