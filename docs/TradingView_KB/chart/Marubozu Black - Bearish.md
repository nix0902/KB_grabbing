- [ Help Center ](/) - / - / Chart - / [ Learn more about candlestick patterns ](/support/folders/43000570503-learn-more-about-candlestick-patterns/) - / [ Marubozu Black - Bearish ](/support/solutions/43000583785-marubozu-black-bearish/) # Marubozu Black - Bearish This is a red candlestick that has no shadow extending from the body either at the open or the close. In Japanese, the name means “close-cropped” or “close-cut.” The candlestick can also be referred to as Bald or Shaven Head. The bullish version of this pattern is the [Marubozu White candlestick pattern](https://www.tradingview.com/chart/?solution=43000583786).
