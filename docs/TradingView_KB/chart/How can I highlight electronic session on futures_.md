By default, electronic session is enabled on futures symbols. You can see the highlighted area on intraday intervals, it marks electronic trading hours. To disable this highlighting or change its color, open Chart settings -&gt; Symbol tab and choose the color and/or opacity for Electronic trading hours background setting.
