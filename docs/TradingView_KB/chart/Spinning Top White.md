### 1. **Candlestick Chart**  
The main element is a candlestick chart with vertical grid lines (for time/price levels) and horizontal grid lines (for price values). Each candlestick represents price action over a period (e.g., 1 hour, 1 day):  
- **Green candles**: Close price > Open price (bullish, upward movement).  
- **Red candles**: Close price < Open price (bearish, downward movement).  
- **Wicks/shadows**: Thin lines extending above/below the candle body, showing the high/low price of the period.  


### 2. **Annotation: “Spinning Top White”**  
A dark gray tooltip box (with a “STW” label in a gray arrow) explains the **Spinning Top White** candlestick pattern:  
- **Definition**: Small, green-bodied candlesticks with upper/lower shadows (wicks) longer than the body.  
- **Interpretation**: Signals indecision between buyers and sellers (neither side has clear control).  


### 3. **UI Elements**  
- **“STW” Label**: A small gray box with an upward arrow, marking the specific candlestick being annotated.
