It may not be very intuitive at the moment, but you can currently disable executions and execution labels in Bar Replay mode through the Trading settings. To do this: - Open Settings in TradingView. - Navigate to the Trading tab. - Turn off Executions and Execution Labels . This will prevent executions and their labels from appearing on the chart while you use Bar Replay . We are working on expanding trading settings for Bar Replay mode. In the future, most trading preferences will be directly integrated into Replay mode, making the experience more consistent.
