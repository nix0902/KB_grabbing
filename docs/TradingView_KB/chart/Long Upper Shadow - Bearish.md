To indicate buyer domination of the first part of a session, candlesticks will present with long upper shadows, as well as short lower shadows, consequently raising bidding prices. The bullish version of this pattern is the [Long Lower Shadow pattern](https://www.tradingview.com/chart/?solution=43000583783).
