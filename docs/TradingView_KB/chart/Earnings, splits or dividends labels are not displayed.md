To do this, open Chart Settings, go to the Events tab and check the status of your display settings: - If your chart looks too compressed, you can zoom in for a more spacious environment. If a chart is heavily compressed, we hide the dividend, split and earning labels so that they do not make your chart too cluttered or cause any difficulties for you while you work.
