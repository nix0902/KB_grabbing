To configure the display mode of currency labels and units of measurement on your chart: - Open chart settings . - Go to the Scales and Lines tab in the menu that appears. - Find the Currency and Units menu item, where you can select the desired display mode. - Once you have selected, save your changes by clicking the OK button.
