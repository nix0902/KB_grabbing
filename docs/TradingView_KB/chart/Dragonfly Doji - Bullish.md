This is a specific Doji where both the open and close price are at the high of a given day or are relatively close to it. A dragonfly doji with a longer lower shadow signals a bullish trend. The bearish version of the Dragonfly Doji pattern is the [Gravestone Doji pattern](https://www.tradingview.com/?solution=43000583773).
