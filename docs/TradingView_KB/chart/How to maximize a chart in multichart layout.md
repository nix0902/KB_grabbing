### **Top Bar (Global Controls)**  
- **Left**: Zoom (+/-), Timeframe (D for 1D), Trade/Chart toggle, *Indicators* (dropdown), *Alert*, *Replay* (dropdown), Back/Forward navigation.  
- **Right**: *My layout* (dropdown), Timeframe shortcuts (D, U, U, Y), Theme toggle, Settings, Screenshot, *Publish* (share chart).  


### **Chart Header**  
- **Symbol**: `Apple Inc · 1D · NASDAQ` (with Apple logo).  
- **Price Data**: `O211.56 H218.84 L211.28 C218.27 +4.17 (+1.95%)` (open, high, low, close, change, % change).  
- **Bid/Ask**: Red `220.40 SELL` (bid) and blue `220.48 BUY` (ask) with 0.08 spread.  
- **Volume**: `Vol 94.13M` (94.13 million shares).  


### **Charts (Candlestick + Volume)**  
- **Candlesticks**: Green = closing > opening (bullish), Red = closing < opening (bearish).  
- **Volume Bars**: Pink = bearish (close < open), Teal = bullish (close > open).  
- **Right Chart (Zoomed)**: Red arrow highlights a downward trend (e.g., price decline).  


### **Left Sidebar (Tools)**  
Icons for:  
- Chart type (line, area, etc.), Trend lines, Fibonacci, Alerts, Text, Drawing tools, Lock, Indicators, Links, Delete.  


### **Bottom Bar**

- **Additional Tools**: *Stock Screener*, *Pine Editor* (scripting), *Strategy Tester*, *Replay Trading*, *Trading Panel*.  


### **Right Sidebar (Info/Actions)**  
Icons for:  
- Notes, History, Alerts, Chat, Fullscreen, Calendar, Split, Maximize chart, Time (09:06:03 UTC), *ADJ* (adjusted prices), Bell (notifications), Help.  


### **Key Data Points**  
- **Pre-market**: `Pre 220.42` (pre-market price).
