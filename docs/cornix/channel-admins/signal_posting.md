The format needs to follow certain posting rules, otherwise the bot will not be able to read and parse it correctly. </p></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-subheading intercom-interblocks-align-left"><h2 id="h_ef74863afa"><br/>Main Format Rules:</h2></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-unordered-nested-list"><ul><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Always include the <b>full coin pair name</b> (for example BTC/USD).</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Always include the &quot;<b>Buy&quot; </b>or<b> &quot;Entry&quot; keyword</b> in the message.</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Always include either the &quot;<b>Sell&quot; </b>or<b> &quot;Stop&quot; keywords</b> in the message.</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>For <b>Take Profit targets</b> you can use either &quot;Take Profit&quot;, &quot;Sell&quot;, &quot;Target&quot;<br/>or &quot;TP&quot;.</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>You can include up to <b>10 Take-profits</b> and up to <b>10 Entry targets</b>.</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>There can only be <b>one Stop Loss</b> target.</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p><b>All targets</b> must be specified as prices and not as percentages. To place targets based on distance percentage, users can set specific entry/ take-profit strategies on their personal Signals Bot.</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>If you include a <b>Trailing Configuration</b>, the formatting must match the example below <b>exactly</b>. Just change the numerical values as needed.</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>A stop loss price <b>can&#x27;t be above</b> the entry price for a long signal, <b>or below</b> the entry price for a short signal.  </p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>It is <b>not obligatory</b> to write LONG or SHORT. Cornix will do this automatically based on the buy and sell prices.</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Try to avoid variations of the keywords, like &quot;S/L&quot; etc. Instead, type out the word &quot;Stop&quot;.</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Try to minimize the usage of unicode emojis like a diamond, unnecessary spaces, irrelevant texts etc.</p></div></li></ul></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-horizontal-rule"><hr/></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-subheading intercom-interblocks-align-left"><h2 id="h_149e699b7d">Amount Per Trade:</h2></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-unordered-nested-list"><ul><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>It is optional to include an &quot;Amount per trade&quot; configuration in your signal. </p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>For the regular &quot;<b>Amount Per Trade as</b> <b>Percentage&quot;</b>, use the following formats:</p></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-unordered-nested-list"><ul><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Amount per trade 5%</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Amount: 45.0%</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Amount 5%</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Invest up to 5% of your portfolio</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Capital invested : 2% 5% Max</p></div></li></ul></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>When setting the amount per trade as <b>&quot;Risk percentage&quot;</b>, use the formats:</p></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-unordered-nested-list"><ul><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Risk management: Risk 1% - 1.5% of your portfolio</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Risk management 0.5% </p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>RM: 10.0%</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Amount: 1.0% Risk</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Any other combination that includes the text &quot;Risk Management&quot; or &quot;RM&quot;</p></div></li></ul></div></li></ul></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-subheading3 intercom-interblocks-align-left"><h3 id="h_b5b1180f36"><b>❗Important Notes: </b></h3></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-unordered-nested-list"><ul><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>The limit for this setting is <b>up to 20%</b> amount per trade or 20% risk percentage.</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>When setting a range for the amount, or multiple options for amounts, only one option will be parsed with the signal. It will be the <b>first</b> one mentioned on your text.<br/>For example:<br/>​<i>&quot;Use between 3% and 4% of your portfolio&quot;</i> In this case the bot will take 3% amount per trade.</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>The word &quot;Risk&quot; alone will <b>not</b> get parsed as &quot;Risk percentage&quot;, therefore you must include the full text of &quot;Risk management&quot; or &quot;RM&quot;.</p></div></li></ul></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-horizontal-rule"><hr/></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-subheading intercom-interblocks-align-left"><h2 id="h_127824bf52">Prices</h2></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-unordered-nested-list"><ul><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Prices in the <b>Satoshi</b> <b>range</b> can be abbreviated and will work fine,<br/>eg. 0.00030000 can be written as 30000 or 30K.</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Entry prices written as<b> 100-200</b> will be translated into <b>TWO entries</b>. </p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>In order to create an <b>Entry Zone</b>, add the word ‘Zone’ before the prices.<br/>​<b>eg. Entry/ Buy zone: 100-200.</b></p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Multiple prices can be sent in one line. They will be sorted automatically based on the trade&#x27;s/signal&#x27;s direction.<br/>eg. <b>Buy 100 300 200 400</b>.</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>To buy at the current price use the text <b>&quot;Buy at current price&quot;</b>, for example:</p></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>BTCUSDT</p></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Buy at current price</p></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Sell at 80000 - 81000</p></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Stop at 50000<br/>​<b>❗Note:</b> this will not generate a market order for the trades, instead it will create a limit buy order with the current market price. </p></div></li></ul></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-horizontal-rule"><hr/></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-subheading intercom-interblocks-align-left"><h2 id="h_0ebc70a9ef">Breakout Signals:</h2></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-unordered-nested-list"><ul><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Breakout signals will be triggered when adding the words &#x27;<b>above</b>&#x27; (for long) or &#x27;<b>below</b>&#x27; (for short) for your entries. <br/>eg. <b>Enter above 200-100</b> or <b>Enter Below 100-200</b>. This will wait before buying, making it a Breakout signal.</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Another way to define s Breakout signal is to specify &quot;Signal Type: Breakout&quot; (by default signal is parsed as &quot;Signal Type: Regular&quot;)</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Breakout signals can only include <b>up to 2 entry prices</b>. </p></div></li></ul></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-horizontal-rule"><hr/></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-subheading intercom-interblocks-align-left"><h2 id="h_8871e37083">Leveraged Signals:</h2></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-unordered-nested-list"><ul><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Leverage signals should have a leverage indication, it can be one or more of these 3 options: </p></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-ordered-nested-list"><ol><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>The keyword &#x27;<b>Leverage</b>&#x27; or &#x27;<b>Lev</b>&#x27; and then the desired leverage type &amp; level.</p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>The leverage type &amp; level alone. eg. <b>Cross x20</b>/ <b>Isolated x5</b>. </p></div></li><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Mentioning the <b>leveraged</b> <b>exchanges names</b>, for example: &quot;Exchanges: Bybit USDT, Binance Futures&quot;. In case no leverage type and level are mentioned, the bot will use the default of Isolated x1.   </p></div></li></ol></div></li></ul></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-horizontal-rule"><hr/></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-subheading intercom-interblocks-align-left"><h2 id="h_5545d1c099"> Entry Zone Signals:</h2></div><div class="intercom-interblocks-unordered-nested-list"><ul><li><div class="intercom-interblocks-paragraph no-margin intercom-interblocks-align-left"><p>Admins can specify a <b>buy zone</b> (a range) rather than a fixed entry price.
