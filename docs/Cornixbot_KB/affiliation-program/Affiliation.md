# Affiliation
Written by Hadar Cornix Updated over 3 months agoTable of contents
Referred UsersExisting UsersEarningsPayouts
Under the affiliation section, you can create your referral link, track your referrals and withdraw your shares.

This article will explain the different tabs under the affiliation section:
-
Referred Users (Referral Link)
-
Existing Users (Promo Code)
-
Earnings
-
Payouts
## Referred Users
### Referral Link
To generate your unique referral link, simply click the "Your Referral Link" button on the top right corner of the screen. As a Cornix affiliate, you will earn a commission via every new Cornix user who registers through your link, including their future payments, for up to 25%. Your link will grant your referred users a 10% discount for any yearly subscription on top of any existing discount (except for promos).

In this tab you can track all the referred users who registered to Cornix using your referral link:
-
Registered Users - the number of users registered to Cornix using your referral link. Following registration, users get 14 days of a free trial, starting after the first trade was created, which means the registered users do not stand for paid users.
-
Paid Users - the number of users who registered to Cornix using your referral link and paid for a subscription which entitles you to your share (in case where no refund was made in the following 7 days from the payment).
-
Active Paid Users:
-
on the left is the number of Cornix users who registered through your referral link, paid for a subscription, and still have an active one.
-
On the right is the number of active paid users required for you to complete the current commission tier.
-
Your Tier - your current commission tier is determined based on the current number of active paid users as follows:
-
Tier 1: 0-100 users 20%
-
Tier 2: 101-500 users 22.5%
-
Tier 3: 501+ users 25%
-
Total Earned - the number of confirmed paid users (following 7 days from payment when no refund was made) multiplied by each payment with the relevant share percentage in accordance with your commission tier.
In the table below you can see a list of each referred user, his registration date, customer ID, the amount paid by each user to Cornix (revenue), and your share out of each user's revenue (earned).

## Existing Users
This tab is visible and available only to channel admins.

### Promo Code
If you are a signals channel admin you will also have the option to generate a Promo code to be used by Cornix existing users to renew their subscriptions. you'll receive a 10% commission for every renewal payment of an existing user that uses your code and your code will grant the ones using it a 10% discount for any yearly subscription on top of any existing discount (except for promos).

In this tab, you can track all the Cornix existing users who renewed their subscriptions using your Promo code:
-
Existing Users - the number of Cornix existing users who renewed their subscriptions using your unique promo code.
-
Total Earned - the number of existing users multiplied by each payment multiplied by 10%.

## Earnings
-
Amount Earned - your share in the revenue.
-
Amount Not Confirmed - is the amount pending approval. This amount represents your shares from paid subscriptions that did not pass 7 days from payment, if not refunded, after 7 days these amounts will become confirmed.

## Payouts
Under this tab, you can create your payouts and track your withdrawals.
-
Available to Payouts - this is the amount you can withdraw.
-
Pending Payouts - payouts that were created and pending approval. Approval can take up to a few days.
-
Amounts Awaiting Approval - the sum of all Pending Payouts.
-
Processed Payouts - the number of approved payouts.
-
Paid to Date - is the amount of money that was paid to you.

### Add a Payout Method
In order to add a payment method to withdraw your earning, click the "+Add a Method" button on the right side of the screen and follow the instructions.

### Need a Different Payout Method?
By default, you can only create a Payout request using the PayPal payment method. In case you are not able to use PayPal, you can contact our support and make a Payout request using XRP (crypto) payment method.
Please reach out to our support team for further instructions.

### Withdraw Your Earnings
To create a new payout click the "+ New Payout" button and fill in the requested info. The minimum amount for a withdrawal is $100.
