# Cornix Points
The Cornix's Legacy Referral Plan
Written by Hadar Cornix Updated over a year agoTable of contents
How Do Cornix Points Work?How to Get Your Referral LinkHow to Convert Cornix Points to a Subscription
Important: The Cornix Points plan is part of an old referral system and is only available for some legacy users. New accounts will not have access to this plan and will automatically be enrolled in our Affiliation Plan.
​
## How Do Cornix Points Work?
-
Cornix Points = Each point is equivalent to $1 USD – The points can be used to purchase any Cornix subscription.
-
Referral Reward: When someone joins Cornix using your referral link and purchases a subscription, you'll receive Cornix Points equal to 20% of their subscription value (in USD).
## How to Get Your Referral Link
-
Open the Cornix Trading Bot.
-
Go to Main Menu > Referral.
-
Copy your unique referral link to share with others.
-
Track the number of people who joined and purchased a subscription using your points.
## How to Convert Cornix Points to a Subscription
-
Once you've accumulated enough points to cover the full cost of any subscription, you can convert your points to purchase that plan.
-
To convert points:
-
Open the Cornix Trading Bot.
-
Go to Main Menu > Referral > Convert Points.
Please Note: You can only convert points if you have enough to cover the entire subscription cost.
Remember, Cornix points can only be used to purchase a subscription, any other payment such as an upgrade, API Slots etc, it not part of the Cornix Points plan.

### Looking for the New Referral Plan?
If you're a newer user or want to learn more about our current referral system, check out our updated Affiliation Plan.
