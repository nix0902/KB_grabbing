# Upgrading our Subscription Plans and Pricing (January 2025)
Written by Hadar Cornix Updated over 10 months ago
Since launching in 2019, Cornix has innovated and focused on building the best crypto auto trading experience for you and the rest of our community. We have continually released new features and enhancements to our platform. From automatically following signal groups to Signals Bots, DCA Bots, TradingView Bots, Grid Bots and many more...

We’ve come to a decision to upgrade our subscription plans to make each one of our plans even more effective and usage based, to increase the value that you get from our platform.

The main change is the new 'UNLIMITED SIGNAL TRADES' that is now available for Advanced members as well, in addition to the Premium plan members. With this change all of our plans will have unlimited access to automated trading, regardless of your subscription type.
*The Free package still offers up to 5 active signal trades.
