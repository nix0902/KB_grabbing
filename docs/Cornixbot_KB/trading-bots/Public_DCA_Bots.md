# Public DCA Bots
What's the 'Public Bots' page, and how to copy a public DCA Bot
Written by Hadar Cornix Updated over 3 months ago
Under the 'Public Bots' page on the DCA section, you can explore and view anonymized public DCA bots that were published by other Cornix users and easily copy them to let them trade for you.

For each bot, you will see its profit from the last month, average monthly profit, and total accumulated profit. To explore bots that match your interests, use the ‘Filters’ on the top right side of the screen. You can filter the public bots by exchanges, symbols, direction (long\short), or their running days:


-
The ‘POPULAR’ tag is attached to bots that have been copied by 5 or more Cornix users since they were published.

Easily copy a bot by clicking the 'Copy Bot' button on the top right corner of each bot:

After clicking the 'Copy Bot' button for any specific bot you will be able to change the bot's general settings such as its name, exchange account, symbol, and amount per trade, to make it suit your own needs (Learn more about the bot's general parameters) and view the bot's configuration by using the "Show Bot Configuration" button.

Then you will be able to choose between copying the bot as it is by clicking the 'Create Bot' button or editing the bot by clicking the 'Edit Bot' button:


If you wish to make more changes by using the 'Edit Bot' button, you will be able to set and change all of the bot's parameters. Learn more on how to configure the bot in our Create your own DCA Bot article.
