# Share and Copy Bots
Seamlessly share and copy trading strategies
Written by Hadar Cornix Updated over 3 months agoTable of contents
How To Share Your BotHow To Copy A Shared Bot
The Cornix bots sharing feature allows you to share your favorite trading bots with other Cornix members. The shared bots can be easily copied by other Cornix users, offering the ability to easily create bots that are identical or similar to the original shared bot.

Bot sharing is available for all Cornix bot types, including Signals, Grid, DCA and TradingView bots.

# How To Share Your Bot
-
Navigate to the desired Bots page and choose the bot you wish to share.
-
Click the three dots menu '...' and select the “Share” option:

-
The bot share link will be copied to your clipboard:
-
Share the link with other Cornix members.
# How To Copy A Shared Bot
-
Click the share link that was provided by the bot owner. This will take you to the Cornix website, where you can view general information and statistics about the bot's historical performance.
-
By default, Cornix will select the account that matches the original bot’s exchange. If that exchange is not available or you don’t have enough funds in that account, another account will be selected. You can change the account according to your needs.
-
You can view the full shared bot configuration by clicking the "Show Bot Configuration" button, and edit the bot’s advanced configuration by clicking the "Edit Bot" button.
-
After changing any of the parameters, or keeping then as they are, click the "Create Bot" button. In the modal, choose whether you want to "create and activate" the bot immediately, or "create only" so the bot will be created as inactive.
-
Click "Save", and your new bot will be created.
