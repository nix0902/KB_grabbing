The Cornix TradingView Bot is an advanced automation tool that lets you execute trades directly from your TradingView alerts. By connecting your alerts to the bot, you can create custom strategies, automate trades, and improve your trading efficiency with minimal manual effort.

Go to My Trading View Bots Page

# How TradingView Bots Work
TradingView Bots integrate your TradingView alerts with Cornix Bots. You can set up alerts based on technical indicators, price movements, or custom Pine scripts. When an alert is triggered, the bot executes the trade automatically on your exchange account.

# Key Features
## 1. Customizable Trade Execution
-
Set alerts based on indicators, trend lines, or price movements.
-
Configure entry, stop-loss, and take-profit levels for each trade.
## 2. Seamless Integration with Cornix
-
Easily link your TradingView alerts to Cornix by using your Bot's UUIDs for Trade Opening and for Trade Closing.
## 3. Supports Long and Short Trades
-
Execute both long and short trades based on predefined alerts.
## 4. Advanced Risk Management
-
Set stop-loss and take-profit levels for each trade.
-
Utilize trailing stop and trailing take-profit strategies to lock in profits.
## 5. Fully Automated Trading
-
No need for manual order execution.
-
Trade execution occurs automatically when alerts are triggered.



# How to Set Up a Trading View Bot
Getting started with Cornix TradingView Bots is simple. Follow these steps to begin automating your trades efficiently:
-
Create a Trading View Bot
-
Connect your Trading View alerts to your bot

# Why Use the Cornix Trading View Bot?
The TradingView Bot offers a powerful automation tool for traders, enhancing efficiency and precision. It eliminates the need for constant market monitoring, reduces emotional trading, and ensures trade opportunities are never missed.
Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced trader seeking advanced customization, the Cornix TradingView Bot is a valuable addition to your trading strategy.
