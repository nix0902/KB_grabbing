# Watchlist
Written by Dor Updated over 4 years ago
In the Watchlist tab, you can track your favorite coins price and change in the last 24 hours! You can add to your list any coin you want that exists in our supported exchanges.
This tab provides you an easy access to the coins you're interested in from the full coins list so you will always stay updated on the market status!
Click on the plus button to add another symbol to your watchlist!
Click on the icon at the top right corner and choose the convenient display for you (boxes / table).
Click long press on the symbol to remove it from your watchlist:
