# Markets
Written by Dor Updated over 4 years ago
In the markets section, you can always stay updated on the market status!
You can see in this section lists of coins in each exchange we support, including trading volume and prices and get detailed information about the different exchanges.
You can also set up a watchlist to keep track of your favorite coins in the most convenient and accessible way!
* Continue to the following articles in this section for more details on each tab.
Note that you can use the following accessorizes for your convenience:
You can always click on the magnifying glass icon to search for the symbol you are interested in.
The prices on the app are displayed in USD by default. Clicking on the Bitcoin icon will convert all prices in the current page to BTC.
