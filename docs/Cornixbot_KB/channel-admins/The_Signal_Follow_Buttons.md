# The Signal Follow Buttons
Understanding the Signal Follow Buttons in Your Telegram Channel
Written by Hadar Cornix Updated over 10 months agoTable of contents
1. View Signal2. One Click Follow3. Set Auto-Trading📌 Important Notes
When a signal is posted in your Telegram channel, three interactive buttons will appear beneath it. Each button offers a different way to engage with the signal or with your channel.


## 1. View Signal
Clicking this button opens the Signal Details page in the Cornix Telegram Mini App.
Here, users can:
-
Review the full signal information
-
Track its real-time progress
-
Click "Set Auto-Trading" to create a Signals Bot for your channel

📝 This page provides a full overview of the signal, inside the Telegram mini app.

## 2. One Click Follow
This button allows users to instantly open a trade based on the signal, with just one click.
-
The trade will be executed using the user's 'Telegram One-Click Follow default' settings for your channel
-
This button is only relevant for users who do not already have an active Signals Bot for your channel.

📝 Here's how to set a One Click Default Signals Bot for a channel in the Signals Bots page.
##
3. Set Auto-Trading
Clicking this button takes the user directly to the "Create Signals Bot" page in the mini app.
-
Here, they can configure a Signals Bot that automatically follows all new signals from your channel.
-
Once activated, new signals will be followed without the need for further actions.
📝 Here's the 'Create Signals Bot' page on the Cornix Telegram mini app.

## 📌 Important Notes
-
If the user already has an active Signals Bot, signals will be followed automatically, there's no need to use "One Click Follow."
-
If the user sets up a new Signals Bot via one of these buttons, it will only apply to future signals, the current signal will not be opened by the bot.
-
Manual traders can continue using "One Click Follow" to act on specific signals without enabling auto-trading.
