# How to Install the Cornix App Within Your Whop Business
Step-by-step guide for connecting Cornix to your Whop business
Written by Hadar Cornix Updated over 2 months agoTable of contents

# 1 - Create your Whop Business
Once you're on your business account, you can follow each one of these steps at any stage, there's no specific order for the actions.

# 2 - Add the Cornix app
Go to your business page > App store > Search for "Cornix Signals Copy Trading" > Click "Add".


# 3 - Connect Group to Cornix
You can choose to create a group on Cornix, or to connect your Telegram/ Discord group.
You can also connect multiple groups (Cornix group, Telegram and Discord).
Note: After connecting the first group, you can get back to this stage at any time via "My Groups" page > "+Add group".

## Connect Telegram Channel
Add the Cornix bot @cornix_trading_bot as a channel admin, giving all default permissions, and follow the instructions on Telegram.

If your Telegram channel is already integrated with Cornix, continue to this step:

Paste and send the verification code from Whop to your Telegram channel. A confirmation message will be received on the channel- "Channel successfully connected".



## Connect Discord Channel
Click the following invitation link https://www.cornix.io/connect_discord_bot/ to connect Cornix Trading Bot to your Discord server. After clicking the link and being forwarded to Discord, choose the server you wish to connect the bot to. The permissions the bot needs will be set up automatically, just click on the 'Authorize' button and the bot will join your server's channels.
To activate the bot on the desired channel type /activate on the text box of the channel.

If your Discord server is already integrated with Cornix, continue to this step:

Paste and send the verification code from Whop to your Discord server. A confirmation message will be received on the channel- "Channel successfully connected".



## Create Cornix Group
The Cornix group will be managed through the Cornix Dashboard and does not depend on any external platform. You'll be able to publish signals manually, generate TradingView-based signals, manage your signals and share them with your clients.


## 'My Groups' Page
Each connected group will be displayed on the "My Groups" page, you can see which group is related to which platform by the small icon below (Cornix/Telegram/Discord).

Via the '...' menu you can delete or disconnect groups from your Whop dashboard.


# 4 - Include the Cornix App in Your Product
Include the Cornix app in your existing product by editing your product details > Apps > Tools > Enable the "Cornix Signals Copy Trading" app > Save.

If you still don't have a product, start by creating your product then add the Cornix app to it.
