# How to add the Cornix Trading Bot to your Discord Channel and integrate with Cornix
Simple Steps for Admins to Connect Cornix Trading Bot to a Discord Channel
Written by Hadar Cornix Updated over 2 months agoTable of contents
Step 1: Connect the Cornix Trading Bot to Your Discord ServerStep 2: Activate the Bot and Enable Auto-TradingStep 3: Link Your Cornix Account with Discord
### Notes:
-
In order to activate the bot or later publish signals successfully on the channel, one must be the server creator or have 'Administrator' or 'Manage Messages' server permissions.
-
In order to enable the bot to connect, make sure the "Allow direct messages from server members" is enabled under your Privacy & Safety settings on your User Settings.

# Step 1: Connect the Cornix Trading Bot to Your Discord Server
Click the following invitation link https://www.cornix.io/connect_discord_bot/ to connect Cornix Trading Bot to your Discord server. After clicking the link and being forwarded to Discord, choose the server you wish to connect the bot to. The permissions the bot needs will be set up automatically, just click on the 'Authorize' button and the bot will join your server's channels.

# Step 2: Activate the Bot and Enable Auto-Trading
To activate the bot on the desired channel type /activate on the text box of the channel. This will activate the signal parsing and the auto trading functionalities in your channel and the following message will be received:


From now on, each valid signal that will be posted in your channel can be easily followed by your channel subscribers by using Cornix’s auto-trading functionality (trades would open fully automatically) or manually by the “follow signal” button that will appear under each new signal.

Cornix will also monitor the signals, send updates in the channel when the prices mentioned in the signals were reached on the exchanges, and display the signal's ROI for the respective targets.

# Step 3: Link Your Cornix Account with Discord
Click the 'Connect to Discord' button on the bot's main menu and follow the instructions:
-
Navigate to the Settings page > "Account & Security" > External Accounts > Discord > Click "Connect"


-
Follow the steps on the pop-up window:
-
Step 1: Navigate to the Cornix Trading Bot on Discord via this link.
-
Step 2: Proceed to send the command to the bot.
-
Step 3: Click "Continue"

-
If the action went through successfully, you'll then see your Discord status as "Connected".

That's it!! Now your Discord channel will be visible on your Cornix account.
