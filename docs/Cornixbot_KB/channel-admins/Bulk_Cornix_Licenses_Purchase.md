# Bulk Cornix Licenses Purchase
Acquire and Assign licenses to your channel members
Written by Hadar Cornix Updated over 2 months ago
With the bulk License Menu, you will be able to assign acquired Cornix licenses (subscriptions) to your members as part of an all in all package that you can offer to your potential channel members, or your existing ones!

You can choose from a variety of subscription types to acquire such as Premium, Advanced, or Asset Manager, and for different time durations- either one month or an annual subscription.

Note: this featured can only be enabled on your account by our team, so feel free to reach out to our support team about this by tapping on the chat icon located at the bottom right corner of the page, and we will provide you with all the relevant information.

Purchasing the licenses is available through your Cornix Admin Panel on Telegram.
After you have purchased your licenses, you will be able to assign them either via your admin panel on Telegram, or via your Admin Interface on your web dashboard.
To get there,
Navigate to the 'Users Management' tab > name sure you choose the right channel's name at the top > go to 'Users'.
There, you will be able to check ☑ the name you wish to assign a license to (as seen in the picture below) and click on 'Assign Licenses' to complete the action.
