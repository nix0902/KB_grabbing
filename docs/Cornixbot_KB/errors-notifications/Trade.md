# Trade didn't Open due to Binance Futures Trading Rules Violation
Written by Hadar Cornix Updated over a year ago
### Why Did My Trade Fail to Open with a 'Binance Futures Trading Rules Violation' Notification?
If your trade didn’t open and you received this notification, it means your orders violated one or more of Binance's Futures Trading Rules.
This issue most commonly occurs due to the small-order limitation, which restricts the number of trades below $50. If this limit is exceeded, Binance may impose a temporary ban on your account. The ban duration can vary depending on different factors.
To learn more, visit the official Binance Futures Trading Rules.

### How Can I Fix This?
To avoid this issue in the future, you can:
-
Increase your trade size – adjust your Amount per Trade to exceed Binance's minimum order limitations.
-
Reduce the number of orders on your trade – limit the number of orders so that each order on your trade will be greater than $50, to comply with Binance's trading rules.
