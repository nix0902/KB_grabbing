# Connect your Account to Telegram/ Discord
Connecting your Telegram/ Discord account on the Cornix Onboarding process
Written by Nikita Shirokov Updated over 6 months agoTable of contents
If You're Onboarding a New Cornix AccountTelegram:Discord:If You Already Have a Cornix Account🖥️ Web Dashboard📱 Mobile App
# If You're Onboarding a New Cornix Account
Connecting your external account with Cornix will allow us to detect which Telegram channels or Discord servers you are a member of. Let's see how we can easily complete the connection after choosing your desired Trading Method.

1) After connecting your exchange and choosing your trading method,
click on 'Connect Account'


2) Choose Telegram or Discord and click 'Get UUID'


## Telegram:
3) Click 'Connect Telegram' or send '/connect_web' to your Telegram bot. You'll receive your UUID. Copy the code and paste it back into the text box.


4) Click 'Connect to Telegram'


5) To complete the validation process, click 'Get Code'.
Go back to the Cornix Trading Bot, copy the code, and paste the code into the text box. In addition, enter your Cornix password and click on 'Continue'




## Discord:
To connect your Discord account, follow the instructions on the screen
-
Navigate to the Cornix Trading Bot on Discord
-
Copy and paste the connect_web command that you get from Cornix
-
Once done, click 'Continue'
Note: In order to be able to connect your Discord account, you must be a member of at least one Discord group that is already integrated with Cornix.


❓Having trouble connecting your Discord account to Cornix? Check this article...


# If You Already Have a Cornix Account
In case you have already registered to Cornix using your email, but have not connected your Telegram/ Discord account yet, follow these steps to connect your accounts:

## 🖥️ Web Dashboard
In the Main Menu on the left bar go to 'Settings' > 'Account & Security' > and under the 'External Accounts' section, connect your Telegram or Discord.
Follow the same steps to get your UUID and Security Code!

## 📱 Mobile App
From the bottom panel click 'Menu' > 'Settings & Privacy' > and under the 'External Accounts' section connect your Telegram/ Discord .
Follow the same steps to get your UUID and Security Code!
