# Connect your KuCoin Account (Create API Keys) - Quick Connect ⚡
How to automatically create encrypted and IP-restricted API keys when connecting to KuCoin
Written by Hadar Cornix Updated over 8 months agoTable of contents
Step 1 - Navigate to your Web DashboardStep 2 - Select the Exchange that you want to connectStep 3 - Choose the sub-exchanges you would like to connectStep 4 - 'Quick Connect'
## Step 1 - Navigate to your Web Dashboard
Under 'Portfolio' select 'Accounts' and tap on '+Create'.


## Step 2 - Select the Exchange that you want to connect
Click 'KuCoin'
​

## Step 3 - Choose the sub-exchanges you would like to connect
Note: By default, all sub exchanges types are checked. Uncheck the ones that you do not want to connect.
In case a sub exchange type you selected does not exist in your account yet, we will direct you to KuCoin in order to create one.


## Step 4 - 'Quick Connect'
Choose 'Quick Connect', give a name to your group of accounts (by default the name will be "My KuCoin"), and click 'Connect to Binance'
You can also follow the instructions on the left side of the page:

Quick connect an API with KuCoin
1. Click Connect to KuCoin
2. Login at KuCoin
3. Click ‘Continue’ when prompted


Once you click ' Connect to KuCoin' you'll be directed to KuCoin Log in page.
Go through the login and authentication steps and you'll be requested to allow Cornix with an Account Access.
Click 'Confirm', and the new accounts will be automatically created on your Cornix account, ready to start trading!
