# Connect your ByBit Account (Create API Keys) - Quick Connect ⚡
How to automatically create encrypted and IP-restricted API keys when connecting to ByBit
Written by Hadar Cornix Updated over 8 months agoTable of contents
Step 1 - Navigate to your Web DashboardStep 2 - Select the Exchange that you want to connectStep 3 - Choose the sub-exchanges you would like to connectStep 4 - 'Quick Connect'Step 5 - Log in to your ByBit Account
## Step 1 - Navigate to your Web Dashboard
Under 'Portfolio' select 'Accounts' and tap on '+Create'.


## Step 2 - Select the Exchange that you want to connect
Click 'ByBit'


## Step 3 - Choose the sub-exchanges you would like to connect
Note: By default, all sub exchanges types are checked (ByBit Spot, ByBit USDT and ByBit Inverse). Uncheck the ones that you do not want to connect.
In case a sub exchange type you selected does not exist in your account yet, we will direct you to ByBit in order to create one.


## Step 4 - 'Quick Connect'
Choose 'Quick Connect'.
Give a name to your group of accounts (by default the name will be "My ByBit")
Click 'Connect to ByBit'

You can also follow the instructions on the left side of the page:
1. Click Connect to ByBit
2. Login at ByBit
3. Click ‘Continue’ when prompted



## Step 5 - Log in to your ByBit Account
You will now be directed to ByBit Log in page.

Once you log in and complete the verification steps, the new ByBit accounts will be automatically created on your Cornix account!
If everything went through successfully, you'll receive a pop up notification at the top of your screen, and 3 new accounts will be available to start trading.
