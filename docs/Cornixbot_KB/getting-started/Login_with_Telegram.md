# Login with Telegram
Login to your Cornix account with your Telegram account
Written by Hadar Cornix Updated over 8 months ago
From now on you can log in to your Cornix account using your Telegram account for verification (phone number).

### 1 - On the Login page, click the Telegram Icon

### 2 - Enter your location and phone number in the international format

### 3 - You'll receive an authorization message from Telegram, click 'Confirm'


### 4 - Click 'Accept' to confirm the terms on your Cornix Dashboard


### 5 - You'll receive confirmation messages from Telegram
Note: There's no need to click the Telegram messages. From this moment on you can freely login to your Cornix account using your Telegram account - any time!


You're all set!
