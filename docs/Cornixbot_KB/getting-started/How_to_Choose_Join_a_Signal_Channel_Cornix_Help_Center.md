# How to Choose & Join a Signal Channel
 
A useful guide for how to choose a signal channel to follow and where to start
         Written by  Cristian Marin   Updated over 8 months ago          Table of contents   
       1) Choose a Channel    2) Contact the Channel Admin    3) Join the Telegram Channel    What If The Channel Doesn't Appear Under Your Account?    ⚠️ Only Cornix Integrated Channels Can be Auto-Traded        
Cornix supports a wide network of  free and paid signal channels  that you can join and trade with automatically using our platform.
  
## 
1) Choose a Channel
  
You can browse available signal channels on the   Cornix Marketplace  , accessible via your web dashboard and mobile app under the  “Marketplace”  tab.
  
In the Marketplace, you can:
   -  
Filter and sort channels by  exchange, pricing, risk level and more 
 
-  
Review each channel’s  performance statistics 
 
-  
Read  user reviews and ratings  to help you choose the right one

   Read more about Cornix Marketplace   
   
 Note:  Not all Cornix integrated channels appear on the Cornix Marketplace. There are many channels out there that have integrated Cornix for auto-trading, and are not listed on our official Marketplace. Make sure to do your own research and get to the one that best fits your strategy and goals. 

## 2) Contact the Channel Admin
  
Once you’ve found a channel you’re interested in, reach out to the  channel admin .  When using the Cornix Marketplace, contact details are available in the  Marketplace  or directly at  cornix.io/partners .
  
If the channel is  paid , any payment or subscription process is handled directly with the channel admin, not via Cornix. 

## 3) Join the Telegram Channel
  
The admin will provide you with an invitation  link to their Telegram channel . After clicking it, you’ll be redirected to their signals group, where you can start viewing and following their signals.

To start trading automatically with these signals, follow our step-by-step guide on  How to Use the Cornix Signals Bot .

## What If The Channel Doesn't Appear Under Your Account?
  
If after joining the Telegram group, you still do not see the channel under ' My Groups ' page on your account, try searching for it by using the 'Missing a Group' button at the top right of the screen. 

If you still do not find the channel, follow these steps:
   -  
Go to the Telegram signals channel.
 
-  
Locate the last signal message.
 
-  
Click the  “Set Auto Trading”  button under the signal.
  
This will open the Cornix setup menu to  create your Signals Bot  for this channel. 

## ⚠️ Only Cornix Integrated Channels Can be Auto-Traded
  
Cornix only works on signal channels that have integrated the Cornix Bots.
  
If they are integrated with Cornix, the three follow buttons ( View Signal ,  One Click Follow  and  Set Auto Trading ) will appear below each one the signals, as follows:
​
       
📖   Read more about the Cornix Follow Buttons  

If the Telegram channel  doesn't have the Cornix Follow buttons  below their signals, it means the channel is not yet integrated with Cornix.
  
In that case, you can contact the channel admin and let them know about Cornix.
The Cornix integration is  free  for admins and makes it easier for users like you to automatically follow their signals, using Cornix!
