# Register and Set Your Account
 
How to easily connect your exchange account with Cornix and start trading
         Written by  Hadar Cornix   Updated over 8 months ago          Table of contents   
       Step 1 - Registration Page     Step 2 - Verify Your Email Address    Step 3 - Create Account - Connect an Exchange     Step 4 - Choose a Trading Method        

## Step 1 - Registration Page 
  
To start the registration process, navigate to the  Cornix Web Dashboard .
  
Start creating your account by inserting your information as shown below: 

##  Step 2 - Verify Your Email Address
  
 A verification code will be sent to your Email. Please copy and pate it on this page. 
       
## Step 3 - Create Account - Connect an Exchange 
  
To connect your desired exchange to Cornix, please select the exchange that you wish to connect and follow the instructions on the left side of your screen. 

   How to Create an Exchange Account in Cornix    
If you have not yet created an account on the exchange, there is a link provided to take you directly to the exchange's website where you can create one.

If at this point you still do not want to link your exchange account, you can start with a Demo Account, by clicking "Continue with a Demo Account"

   Demo Accounts - Risk-Free Trading   

## Step 4 - Choose a Trading Method
  
 Choose between  "Follow a Group"  and  "Copy a DCA Bot" . 
   -  
The  DCA Bots  method will provide you with the option to follow and activate pre-configured bots that are designed to succeed in various market conditions. The public-proven DCA bots were created by other Cornix users.

-  
The  Signals Group  method will provide the option to automatically follow expert traders' signal recommendations by following a channel of your choice. Following a signals group will open new trades for you automatically every time a signal is published. 

 Important Notes:  
   -  
After Choosing your trading method, you will have the option to connect your Telegram/Discord account. Tap  here  to learn how to do so. 
 
-  
Those stages are not obligatory, you can skip them by clicking  "I want to explore instead" , and get back to them on a later stage.

###  Method Settings  
  
Here you will find various options for  DCA  bots and  Signal Channels  to follow. 
  
You will be able to see, among other data points, the average monthly and total profits of the DCA bots and the number of subscribers, and the average signal posting for a channel. These statistics will help you choose the best option to start with.    

### Overview 
  
Choose the exchange you want to trade with and choose your 'Amount per Trade' configuration.
  
Once you set the bots, auto-trading will be activated immediately. 
  
Please know you can go back to your Bot's settings at any time, enable or disable them, and edit their trading configurations. 

That's it, you're all set 👍
