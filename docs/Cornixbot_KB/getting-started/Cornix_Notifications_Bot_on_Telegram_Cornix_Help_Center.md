# Cornix Notifications Bot on Telegram
 
Get real-time trade updates and bot activity alerts directly in your Telegram app
         Written by  Hadar Cornix   Updated over 8 months ago          Table of contents   
       🔧 Manage Notification Preferences    💡 Telegram Mini App Users - What’s Different?    📌 Pin the Notifications Bot        
The  Cornix Notifications Bot  on Telegram sends you real-time updates about your account activity. You’ll receive alerts for every action the bot takes and for any status changes to your trades.
  
 Note:  The notifications bot is only available on Telegram and not available on Discord. 

# 🔧 Manage Notification Preferences
  
To control which notifications you receive:
   -  
Log in to your  Cornix Web Dashboard 
 
-  
Go to the   Notifications Management   page
 
-  
Enable or disable specific notification types based on your preferences

 Important: 
   -  
If you enable  Telegram notifications , make sure to  activate the Notifications Bot  by starting a conversation with   @cornix_notifications_bot   in Telegram.
 
-  
If you do not wish to receive notifications via Telegram, be sure to  disable Telegram notifications  in your dashboard settings to avoid duplicate messages.

# 💡 Telegram Mini App Users - What’s Different?
  
If you registered through the  Cornix Mini App on Telegram , you won't be asked to activate the Notifications Bot during onboarding.
  
Instead, your first notification will appear directly in the  Cornix Trading Bot  chat. 

At the bottom of each notification you'll see this test:
  
 Start a conversation with  @cornix_notifications_bot  in order to avoid receiving notifications in this chat. 

 To receive the notifications in the dedicated bot , open a chat with   @cornix_notifications_bot   and click  Start .

# 📌 Pin the Notifications Bot
  
 Pro Tip:  We recommend  pinning  the Cornix Notifications Bot chat (@cornix_notifications_bot) in your Telegram app for easy and quick access.
