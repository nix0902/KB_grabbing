# Extend Subscription
How to renew or extend your Cornix subscription
Written by Hadar Cornix Updated over a week agoTable of contents
If not on auto-renewal, you will get notifications a week and a day before your subscription ends. We recommend renewing your subscription before it expires to keep all automated updates up and running. Any additional subscription period purchased will be added on top of your current subscription duration.

You can easily renew your subscription through our web application and through Cornix's Telegram bot:

## Web Interface:
Navigate to the Billing section and click 'Purchase Subscription' or 'Extend' if you already have an active subscription plan.

## Telegram Bot:
You can renew your subscription directly in the Cornix Telegram bot:
Main menu > Subscription > Purchase Subscription.
