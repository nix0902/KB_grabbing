# Billing, Plan and Usage
Check which subscription plan you're in, your plan's usage and billing information
Written by Hadar Cornix Updated over a week agoTable of contents
Web Interface:Plan Usage Billing HistoryTelegram BotUnderstanding your usage
# Web Interface:
You can check your current subscription plan and your usage at any time through the Billing page.
Here, you can see your current subscription plan, your expiration date, your current usage and billing history.

In addition, you can easily extend or upgrade your plan.


## Plan Usage
Your usage panel shows two different types of activities:

Your current active quota usage of manual Trades, active signals bots, active grid bots, active DCA bots, active TradingView bots, monthly Backtesting runs, and the number of Primary API slots in use.

The other being a graph of the number of automated signal trades opened during your subscription and, if existing, the "Trades missed" (the number of such trades that were not opened due to your 'active signal trades' quota reaching the limit during the time when new signals were published.)

## Billing History
Switch to the 'Billing History' tab to view your previous subscription extensions and upgrades.


# Telegram Bot
You can access your current subscription details in the Cornix Telegram Bot under:
​Main Menu → Subscription.
There you can see your current plan and your expiration date as well as the option to extend or upgrade your plan.
Under the 'View Details' button you can see your current quotas and your current usage.

# Understanding your usage
-
Active Manual Trades - The number of Manual Trades currently open created through the 'Trading Terminal' or the Mobile App.
-
Active Signals Bots - The number of Signals Bots currently active on your account.
-
Active Grid Bots - The number of Active Grid Bots.
-
Active DCA Bots - The number of Active DCA Bots.
-
Active TradingView Bots - The number of Active Trading View Bots.
-
Monthly Backtesting Runs - How many Backtesting runs have been used so far out of your total monthly qouta.
-
Primary API Slots - The number of Primary API Slots currently in use (can be viewed in the accounts and groups page).
